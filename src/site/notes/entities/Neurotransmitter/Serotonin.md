# Serotonin

## Overview
Monoamine neurotransmitter regulating mood, sleep, appetite, and gut function. Approximately 95% is produced in the gut. Commonly known as the "feel-good" neurotransmitter, it is central to emotional well-being and is the target of most antidepressant medications.
