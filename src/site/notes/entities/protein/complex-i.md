<h1>Complex I</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>NADH:ubiquinone oxidoreductase, the largest complex of the electron transport chain with 45 subunits. Catalyzes the first step of oxidative phosphorylation by oxidizing NADH and transferring electrons to ubiquinone while pumping 4 protons across the inner mitochondrial membrane.</p>
