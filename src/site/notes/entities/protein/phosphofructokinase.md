<h1>Phosphofructokinase</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Rate-limiting enzyme of glycolysis, catalyzing the phosphorylation of fructose-6-phosphate to fructose-1,6-bisphosphate. The most important regulatory point of glycolytic flux, integrating signals from energy status, pH, and hormones to control glucose metabolism.</p>
