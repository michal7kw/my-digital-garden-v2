# CTNNB1

## Overview
**Beta-Catenin (CTNNB1)** is the central node of the **Wnt signaling pathway**.
- **Absence of Wnt**: Beta-catenin is phosphorylated by the destruction complex (APC, Axin, GSK3-beta), ubiquitinated, and degraded.
- **Presence of Wnt**: The destruction complex is inhibited. Beta-catenin accumulates, enters the nucleus, and activates genes promoting proliferation (e.g., Cyclin D1, c-Myc).

It is also an essential structural component of **Adherens Junctions**, linking E-cadherin to alpha-catenin and the actin cytoskeleton.
