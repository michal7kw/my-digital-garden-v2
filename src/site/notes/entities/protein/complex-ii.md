<h1>Complex II</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Succinate dehydrogenase, the only enzyme that participates in both the TCA cycle and the electron transport chain. Oxidizes succinate to fumarate while reducing FAD to FADH2, transferring electrons to ubiquinone. Notably, it does not pump protons across the membrane.</p>
