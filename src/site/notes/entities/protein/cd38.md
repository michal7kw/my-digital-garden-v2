<h1>CD38</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>A multifunctional ectoenzyme that is the primary NAD+ consuming enzyme in mammals. Functions as NAD+ glycohydrolase, degrading NAD+ and contributing to age-related NAD+ decline. Also involved in calcium signaling and immune cell regulation.</p>
