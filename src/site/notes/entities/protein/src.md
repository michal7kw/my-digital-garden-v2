# SRC

## Overview
**Src (c-Src)** is the prototype of the Src Family Kinases (SFKs). It is typically tethered to the inner face of the plasma membrane by myristoylation.

It is activated by receptor tyrosine kinases (like EGFR) and G-protein coupled receptors. Once activated, it phosphorylates focal adhesion kinase (FAK) and other substrates to promote:
1.  **Cell Migration**: Remodeling the actin cytoskeleton.
2.  **Cell Survival**: Activating the PI3K-Akt pathway.
3.  **Cell Proliferation**: Activating the MAPK pathway.
