<h1>BDNF Gene</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The BDNF gene encodes brain-derived neurotrophic factor, the most abundant neurotrophin in the brain. BDNF supports neuronal survival, promotes synaptic plasticity, and is essential for learning and memory (especially in the hippocampus). The Val66Met polymorphism (rs6265, G&gt;A) affects ~30% of the population — Met carriers have impaired activity-dependent BDNF secretion, smaller hippocampal volumes, and altered stress reactivity. Met carriers may benefit more from exercise (which robustly increases BDNF) and show differential responses to antidepressants.</p>
