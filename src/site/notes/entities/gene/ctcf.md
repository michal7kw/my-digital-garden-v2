<h1>CTCF</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The <strong>CTCF</strong> gene encodes an 11-zinc finger transcription factor that acts as the &quot;master weaver&quot; of the genome. It binds to tens of thousands of sites in the genome to regulate 3D chromatin structure.</p>
<p>Its primary roles are:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Insulator</strong>: Blocking the interaction between enhancers and promoters.</li>
<li><strong>Loop Formation</strong>: Creating Topologically Associating Domains (TADs) by anchoring chromatin loops.</li>
</ol>
