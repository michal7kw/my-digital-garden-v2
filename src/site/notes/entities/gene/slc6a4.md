<h1>SLC6A4</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>SLC6A4 encodes the serotonin transporter (SERT), which clears serotonin from the synaptic cleft — the direct target of SSRIs. The 5-HTTLPR promoter polymorphism has short (S) and long (L) alleles: S/S carriers have reduced SERT expression with higher synaptic serotonin at baseline but increased vulnerability to stress-related depression (gene × environment interaction). L/L carriers have more efficient serotonin clearance and may respond better to SSRIs. The rs25531 A&gt;G variant further modifies L allele function (L_G behaves like S).</p>
