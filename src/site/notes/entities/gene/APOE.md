# APOE

## Overview
The **APOE** gene exists in three common alleles:
1.  **ε3** (Neutral): Most common (~78%).
2.  **ε4** (Risk): Increases Alzheimer's risk 3x (heterozygous) to 12x (homozygous). Impairs Amyloid Beta clearance.
3.  **ε2** (Protective): Rare (~7%), reduces Alzheimer's risk.
