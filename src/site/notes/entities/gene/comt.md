<h1>COMT</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>COMT encodes catechol-O-methyltransferase, which degrades catecholamines (dopamine, norepinephrine, epinephrine) and catechol estrogens. The Val158Met polymorphism (rs4680) creates two distinct phenotypes: Val/Val (&quot;warrior&quot;) has 3-4x higher enzyme activity with rapid dopamine clearance — better stress resilience but lower baseline PFC dopamine; Met/Met (&quot;worrier&quot;) has lower activity with higher PFC dopamine — better working memory but increased anxiety and pain sensitivity. This polymorphism is one of the most studied in psychiatric and cognitive genetics.</p>
