<h1>Growth Hormone</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>191-amino acid protein hormone secreted by somatotroph cells of the anterior pituitary. Primary regulator of linear growth, body composition, and metabolic substrate utilization. Stimulates IGF-1 production, promotes lipolysis, spares protein during fasting, and exhibits context-dependent effects on glucose metabolism.</p>
