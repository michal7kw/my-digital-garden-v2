<h1>Globus Pallidus</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Major output nucleus of the basal ganglia, divided into external (GPe) and internal (GPi) segments. GPi is the primary output to thalamus, providing tonic inhibition that is selectively released to permit voluntary movement. GPe modulates the indirect pathway. Target for deep brain stimulation (DBS) in dystonia and Parkinson's disease.</p>
