<h1>Habenula</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Small epithalamic structure acting as the brain's &quot;disappointment center&quot; — it encodes negative reward prediction errors, the opposite of VTA dopamine neurons. The lateral habenula fires when expected rewards fail to materialize, inhibiting dopamine and serotonin release. Hyperactive in depression (learned helplessness model). Emerging target for DBS in treatment-resistant depression. Mediates the aversive properties of nicotine withdrawal.</p>
