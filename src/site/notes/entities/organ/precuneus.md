<h1>Precuneus</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Medial parietal lobe region involved in visuospatial imagery, episodic memory retrieval, and self-awareness. One of the highest metabolic rates of any brain region. A core node of the default mode network. Among the earliest regions showing metabolic decline and amyloid accumulation in preclinical Alzheimer's disease.</p>
