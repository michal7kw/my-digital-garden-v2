<h1>Striatum</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Major input structure of the basal ganglia, comprising the caudate nucleus and putamen (dorsal striatum) and nucleus accumbens (ventral striatum). Receives glutamatergic input from cortex and dopaminergic input from midbrain. The dorsal striatum mediates motor control and habit learning; the ventral striatum (NAc) processes reward and motivation. Primary site of dopamine action in the brain.</p>
