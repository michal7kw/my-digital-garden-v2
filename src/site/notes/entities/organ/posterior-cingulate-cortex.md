<h1>Posterior Cingulate Cortex</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Medial parietal cortex region and a central hub of the default mode network (DMN). Active during self-referential thought, autobiographical memory retrieval, and mind-wandering. Among the first regions to show amyloid deposition in Alzheimer's disease. Deactivation during focused tasks is a marker of cognitive engagement.</p>
