<h1>Superior Colliculus</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Midbrain structure forming the tectum roof, involved in visual orientation and saccadic eye movement control. Receives direct retinal input and mediates reflexive visual attention shifts. Contains a multimodal spatial map integrating visual, auditory, and somatosensory information.</p>
