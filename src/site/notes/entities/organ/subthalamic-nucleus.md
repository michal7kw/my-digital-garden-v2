<h1>Subthalamic Nucleus</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Small lens-shaped nucleus in the basal ganglia circuit, the only glutamatergic (excitatory) structure in an otherwise GABAergic system. Drives the &quot;indirect pathway&quot; that suppresses unwanted movements. The primary target for deep brain stimulation (DBS) in Parkinson's disease — DBS of the STN reduces tremor, rigidity, and bradykinesia, allowing medication reduction.</p>
