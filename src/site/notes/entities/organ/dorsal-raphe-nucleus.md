<h1>Dorsal Raphe Nucleus</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Largest serotonergic nucleus in the brainstem, containing ~235,000 serotonin neurons in humans. Projects to virtually all forebrain regions (cortex, hippocampus, amygdala, basal ganglia, hypothalamus). Primary target of SSRI antidepressants. Modulates mood, anxiety, sleep, appetite, and pain. See also <a href="/entities/anatomy/raphe-nuclei/" class="internal-link">Raphe Nuclei</a> in anatomy for the full raphe system.</p>
