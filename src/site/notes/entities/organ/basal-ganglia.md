<h1>Basal Ganglia</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Group of subcortical nuclei (caudate, putamen, globus pallidus, subthalamic nucleus, substantia nigra) critical for motor control, procedural learning, habit formation, and reward-based decision making. Dysfunction causes Parkinson's disease (dopamine loss in substantia nigra), Huntington's disease (caudate/putamen degeneration), and dystonia.</p>
