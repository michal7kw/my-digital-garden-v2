<h1>Magnesium</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Essential mineral involved in 600+ enzymatic reactions. In the brain, magnesium blocks NMDA receptors at resting potential, preventing excitotoxicity. Magnesium L-threonate (Magtein) is the only form shown to cross the BBB and increase brain Mg levels.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 4/5</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Voltage-dependent NMDA receptor blockade prevents excitotoxicity; enhances synaptic density in hippocampus; supports GABAergic tone; regulates HPA axis stress response.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>200-400mg elemental Mg daily (L-threonate preferred for cognitive effects)</p>
