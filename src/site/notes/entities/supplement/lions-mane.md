<h1>Lion's Mane</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Medicinal mushroom (Hericium erinaceus) containing hericenones and erinacines that stimulate nerve growth factor (NGF) synthesis. One of the few natural compounds shown to promote neurogenesis and remyelination.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 3/5</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Hericenones stimulate NGF synthesis in astrocytes; erinacines cross BBB to promote BDNF expression; supports hippocampal neurogenesis and remyelination.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>500-3000mg daily (fruiting body extract, standardized to hericenones)</p>
