<h1>Vitamin E</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Fat-soluble antioxidant family consisting of tocopherols and tocotrienols. Protects cell membranes from oxidative damage. Full-spectrum forms preferred over synthetic alpha-tocopherol.</p>
