<h1>Vitamin K2</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Fat-soluble vitamin essential for calcium metabolism, directing calcium to bones and away from arteries. Critical for cardiovascular and bone health, often paired with vitamin D.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Activates vitamin K-dependent proteins including osteocalcin (bone) and matrix Gla protein (arteries), directing calcium to bones and preventing arterial calcification.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>100-200 mcg MK-7 daily or 1000-5000 mcg MK-4</p>
