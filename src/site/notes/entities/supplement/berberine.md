<h1>Berberine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Powerful plant alkaloid with profound effects on blood sugar and metabolism. Activates AMPK similar to metformin. One of the most effective natural compounds for metabolic health.</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Berberine activates AMPK (AMP-activated protein kinase), the master cellular energy sensor, through mild inhibition of Complex I of the electron transport chain. This mimics the effects of exercise and caloric restriction. AMPK activation leads to increased glucose uptake (independent of insulin), enhanced fatty acid oxidation, inhibition of gluconeogenesis, and reduced lipogenesis. Berberine also modulates gut microbiome composition, inhibits PCSK9 (lowering LDL cholesterol), and may activate PGC-1α for mitochondrial biogenesis.</p>
