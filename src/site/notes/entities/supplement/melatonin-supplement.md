<h1>Melatonin Supplement</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Supplemental form of the sleep hormone melatonin. Supports sleep onset, jet lag, and circadian rhythm regulation. Also provides antioxidant benefits.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)</p>
<p><strong>Research Summary:</strong> Strong evidence for sleep onset and jet lag. Effective at low doses (0.3-1 mg). Antioxidant benefits at higher doses.</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Binds MT1 and MT2 receptors to signal &quot;biological night.&quot; Regulates circadian rhythm, promotes sleep onset, and provides potent antioxidant protection.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>0.3-5 mg for sleep; higher for other purposes</p>
