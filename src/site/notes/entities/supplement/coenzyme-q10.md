<h1>Coenzyme Q10</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>A vitamin-like substance found in every cell of the body, essential for mitochondrial ATP production and acting as a powerful lipid-soluble antioxidant.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Electron carrier in the mitochondrial electron transport chain (Complex I/II to III) facilitating ATP synthesis; membrane antioxidant protecting lipids from peroxidation.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>100-200 mg/day for general health; up to 300-600 mg for heart failure/neuroprotection. Best absorbed with fat.</p>
