<h1>Chromium</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Essential trace mineral that enhances insulin signaling and glucose metabolism. Chromium picolinate is the most bioavailable form. Important for blood sugar regulation.</p>
<p><strong>Research Summary:</strong> Modest but consistent benefits for glucose metabolism in insulin-resistant individuals. May reduce carbohydrate cravings. Effects more pronounced in those with chromium deficiency or metabolic dysfunction.</p>
