<h1>Quercetin</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Powerful plant flavonoid with antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and senolytic properties. Found in onions, apples, and berries. Activates Nrf2 and inhibits mast cell degranulation.</p>
