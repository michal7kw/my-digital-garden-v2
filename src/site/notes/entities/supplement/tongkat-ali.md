<h1>Tongkat Ali</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Root extract of Eurycoma longifolia, a Southeast Asian medicinal plant traditionally used as an aphrodisiac and energy tonic. Contains bioactive quassinoids (eurycomanone, eurycolactone) that modulate testosterone via HPA axis and SHBG mechanisms. Clinical evidence supports modest testosterone elevation, improved stress hormones, and ergogenic benefits. Also known as Malaysian Ginseng or Longjack.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
<p><strong>Research Summary:</strong> Multiple RCTs support modest testosterone increases (particularly free testosterone via SHBG reduction), improved cortisol-to-testosterone ratio, and ergogenic benefits. Systematic reviews confirm small but consistent effects on testosterone in stressed and aging males. Evidence for fertility improvement (sperm parameters) in several small RCTs. Generally well-tolerated at standard doses.</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Reduces sex hormone-binding globulin (SHBG) levels, increasing free testosterone. Modulates cortisol via HPA axis — lowers cortisol-to-testosterone ratio. Eurycomanone inhibits aromatase (CYP19), reducing estrogen conversion. Activates Leydig cell steroidogenesis. Phosphodiesterase inhibition may contribute to ergogenic effects.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>100-200mg standardized extract (1-2% eurycomanone) daily or 200-400mg hot water extract</p>
