<h1>B-Complex Vitamins</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>B-Complex Vitamins is a comprehensive supplement providing all eight essential B vitamins in a single formulation. These water-soluble vitamins serve as critical cofactors for energy metabolism at virtually every level — from glycolysis in the cytoplasm to oxidative phosphorylation in the mitochondria. Because B vitamins are water-soluble and not stored in significant quantities, daily replenishment is essential for maintaining optimal metabolic function.</p>
<p>The B vitamins work synergistically: for example, thiamine (B1) and niacin (B3) are both required in the TCA cycle, while riboflavin (B2) is needed to regenerate the FAD cofactor. A deficiency in any single B vitamin can create a metabolic bottleneck that impairs the entire energy production chain.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 4/5</p>

<h2>Dosage</h2>
<h3>Standard Supplementation</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Dose</strong>: 1 capsule daily (most formulations provide 100-300% DV)</li>
<li><strong>Form</strong>: Look for active/methylated forms (methylfolate, methylcobalamin, P-5-P, riboflavin-5-phosphate)</li>
<li><strong>Timing</strong>: Morning with food (B vitamins can be energizing)</li>
</ul>
