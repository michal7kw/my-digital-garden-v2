<h1>GABA</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Gamma-aminobutyric acid, the brain's primary inhibitory neurotransmitter. GABA reduces neuronal excitability, promotes relaxation, reduces anxiety, and facilitates sleep. It provides the &quot;brake&quot; to glutamate's &quot;accelerator.&quot;</p>
