<h1>Creatine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Creatine is the most studied sports supplement with an exceptional safety record. It rapidly regenerates ATP during high-intensity exercise and is proven to increase strength, power, and muscle mass. Emerging research supports cognitive benefits, especially under stress.</p>
<p>Evidence Level: 5/5 (Extensively researched, proven effective and safe)</p>
