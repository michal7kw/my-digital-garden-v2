# Creatine

## Overview

Creatine is the most studied sports supplement with an exceptional safety record. It rapidly regenerates ATP during high-intensity exercise and is proven to increase strength, power, and muscle mass. Emerging research supports cognitive benefits, especially under stress.

Evidence Level: 5/5 (Extensively researched, proven effective and safe)
