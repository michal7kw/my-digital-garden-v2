<h1>Vitamin B6</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Essential water-soluble vitamin crucial for neurotransmitter synthesis, amino acid metabolism, and over 100 enzymatic reactions. The active form P5P bypasses liver conversion.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Active form P5P is cofactor for 100+ enzymes including those synthesizing serotonin, dopamine, GABA, and GABA. Essential for amino acid metabolism and hemoglobin synthesis.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>25-100 mg daily; P5P at lower doses</p>
