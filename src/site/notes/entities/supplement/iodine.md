<h1>Iodine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Essential trace element required for thyroid hormone synthesis (T3 and T4). Critical for metabolic regulation, cognitive development, and overall health. Deficiency remains common worldwide.</p>
<p><strong>Research Summary:</strong> Essential for thyroid function. Deficiency causes goiter, hypothyroidism, and developmental issues. Excess can also cause thyroid dysfunction. Balance with selenium important for thyroid health.</p>
