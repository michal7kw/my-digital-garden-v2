<h1>Omega-3 Fatty Acids</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Essential polyunsaturated fatty acids (EPA and DHA) critical for brain structure and function. DHA comprises 40% of polyunsaturated fatty acids in the brain, supporting membrane fluidity, BDNF expression, and anti-inflammatory signaling.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 4/5</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>DHA supports neuronal membrane fluidity and BDNF expression; EPA reduces neuroinflammation via resolvins and protectins; both modulate serotonergic and dopaminergic neurotransmission.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>1-3g combined EPA+DHA daily</p>
