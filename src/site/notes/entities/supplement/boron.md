<h1>Boron</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Trace mineral important for bone health, hormone balance, and cognitive function. May support testosterone levels and reduce inflammatory markers. Essential for calcium, magnesium, and vitamin D metabolism.</p>
<p><strong>Research Summary:</strong> Limited but promising research showing benefits for bone health, hormone optimization (especially in deficient individuals), and cognitive function. Often overlooked but may be particularly important for those with low dietary intake.</p>
