<h1>Tart Cherry Extract</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Tart cherry (Montmorency cherry, Prunus cerasus) extract has become one of the most popular natural recovery supplements in sports nutrition. Its high concentration of anthocyanins and polyphenolic compounds provides potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects that reduce exercise-induced muscle damage markers, decrease delayed onset muscle soreness (DOMS), and accelerate functional recovery.</p>
<p>Multiple randomized controlled trials in marathon runners, cyclists, and strength athletes demonstrate reduced muscle soreness, faster recovery of strength, and lower inflammatory markers (IL-6, CRP, creatine kinase) with tart cherry supplementation around exercise events.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 3/5</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<h3>Anti-Inflammatory</h3>
<ul>
<li>Inhibits COX-1 and COX-2 enzymes (similar mechanism to NSAIDs but gentler)</li>
<li>Reduces prostaglandin E2 and thromboxane B2 production</li>
<li>Decreases NF-kB activation in muscle tissue</li>
<li>Lowers circulating IL-6 and CRP after exercise</li>
</ul>
<h3>Antioxidant</h3>
<ul>
<li>Anthocyanins directly scavenge superoxide, hydroxyl radicals, and peroxynitrite</li>
<li>Protects muscle cell membranes from lipid peroxidation</li>
<li>Reduces protein carbonylation and DNA oxidative damage markers (8-OHdG)</li>
</ul>
<h3>Recovery Effects</h3>
<ul>
<li>Reduces DOMS severity by 13-25% in controlled studies</li>
<li>Accelerates isometric strength recovery after eccentric exercise</li>
<li>Reduces creatine kinase elevation post-exercise</li>
<li>May improve sleep quality (contains natural melatonin)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<h3>Standard Protocol</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Dose</strong>: 480-960 mg/day (capsule) or 30 ml concentrate twice daily</li>
<li><strong>Protocol</strong>: Start 3-4 days before intense exercise event, continue 2 days after</li>
<li><strong>For ongoing training</strong>: 480 mg/day as maintenance</li>
<li><strong>Form</strong>: Concentrate (CherryActive, CherryPure) or freeze-dried capsules</li>
</ul>
<h3>Important Notes</h3>
<ul>
<li>Contains natural sugars (juice form provides ~130 kcal per 240 ml)</li>
<li>Capsule form avoids caloric content</li>
<li>High-dose antioxidant supplementation may blunt training adaptations if used chronically (periodize use)</li>
</ul>
