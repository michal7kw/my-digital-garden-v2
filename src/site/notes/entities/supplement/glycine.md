<h1>Glycine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Conditionally essential amino acid with inhibitory neurotransmitter function. Key component of collagen, glutathione precursor, and important for sleep quality through body temperature regulation.</p>
<p><strong>Research Summary:</strong> Well-established for sleep quality improvement through thermoregulation. Emerging research on metabolic health, methylation, and longevity. Remarkably safe at high doses with sweet taste.</p>
