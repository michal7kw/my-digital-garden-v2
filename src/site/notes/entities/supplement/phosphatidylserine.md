<h1>Phosphatidylserine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Phospholipid that comprises 15% of the brain's phospholipid pool. Essential for neuronal membrane integrity, cell signaling, and neurotransmitter release. One of the few supplements with an FDA-qualified health claim for cognitive function.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 3/5</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Maintains membrane fluidity and receptor function; enhances acetylcholine and dopamine release; supports PKC signaling; modulates cortisol response to stress.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>100-300mg daily</p>
