<h1>Beetroot Extract</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Beetroot extract and juice have emerged as one of the most effective natural ergogenic aids for endurance and moderate-intensity exercise performance. The active component is dietary nitrate (NO3-), which the body converts to nitric oxide (NO) through the nitrate-nitrite-NO pathway. This is distinct from the L-arginine-NOS pathway and operates most effectively under the low-oxygen conditions present in working muscle.</p>
<p>Multiple meta-analyses confirm that beetroot supplementation reduces the oxygen cost of submaximal exercise by 3-5%, extends time to exhaustion by 4-25% (dose-dependent), and can improve time-trial performance by 1-3%. Effects are most pronounced in recreational athletes and at altitudes where oxygen is limited.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 4/5</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<h3>Nitrate → Nitric Oxide Pathway</h3>
<ul>
<li>Beetroot NO3- → NO2- (via oral bacteria, entero-salivary circulation)</li>
<li>NO2- → NO (in acidic stomach + hypoxic tissue conditions)</li>
<li>NO enhances vasodilation → increased blood flow to working muscles</li>
<li>NO improves mitochondrial efficiency → reduced O2 cost per ATP</li>
<li>NO enhances calcium handling → improved muscle contraction efficiency</li>
</ul>
<h3>Performance Effects</h3>
<ul>
<li>Reduces VO2 at submaximal intensities (lower oxygen cost for same work)</li>
<li>Extends time to exhaustion at constant workload</li>
<li>Improves 5-30 km time trial performance</li>
<li>Enhances repeated sprint ability (team sports)</li>
<li>Greater effect at altitude (synergy with hypoxic NO2- → NO conversion)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<h3>Standard Protocol</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Dose</strong>: 6-8 mmol nitrate (~400-500 mg nitrate)</li>
<li><strong>Form</strong>: Concentrated beetroot shot (70 ml) or ~500 ml juice</li>
<li><strong>Timing</strong>: 2-3 hours before exercise (peak nitrite levels)</li>
<li><strong>Loading</strong>: Multi-day supplementation (3-6 days) may provide greater benefits</li>
<li><strong>Note</strong>: Avoid mouthwash (kills oral bacteria needed for nitrate reduction)</li>
</ul>
