<h1>L-Theanine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>L-Theanine is a non-protein amino acid found almost exclusively in Camellia sinensis (tea plant). It readily crosses the blood-brain barrier within 30 minutes, promoting alpha brain wave activity (8-13 Hz) — the signature of relaxed, alert attention. This creates a unique &quot;calm focus&quot; state: anxiolytic without sedation. L-theanine is the compound responsible for tea's relaxing effect despite its caffeine content. When combined with caffeine (as in tea), it enhances focus while smoothing out caffeine's jittery edge — one of the most well-validated nootropic stacks.</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>L-theanine is structurally similar to glutamate. It modulates neurotransmission through multiple mechanisms: (1) partial agonist at glutamate receptors (reduces excitotoxicity), (2) increases GABA, serotonin, and dopamine levels in the brain, (3) promotes alpha brain wave generation (EEG-verified within 40 min of ingestion), (4) inhibits cortical neuron excitation (anxiolytic without sedation), (5) increases BDNF expression (neuroprotective).</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Anxiety/relaxation</strong>: 100-200 mg (equivalent of 5-10 cups of green tea)</li>
<li><strong>Focus (with caffeine)</strong>: 100-200 mg L-theanine + 50-100 mg caffeine (2:1 ratio optimal)</li>
<li><strong>Sleep support</strong>: 200-400 mg 30-60 min before bed (not a sedative — reduces sleep-disrupting anxiety)</li>
<li><strong>Onset</strong>: 30-40 minutes; alpha waves detectable on EEG within this window</li>
<li><strong>Safety</strong>: Excellent safety profile; no known tolerance, dependence, or significant side effects</li>
<li><strong>Forms</strong>: Free-form L-theanine (Suntheanine is the patented, most-studied form)</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 3/5</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Structural analog of glutamate that modulates AMPA and kainate receptors; increases GABA, serotonin, and dopamine levels; promotes alpha wave generation for calm focus.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>100-400mg daily, often combined with caffeine</p>
