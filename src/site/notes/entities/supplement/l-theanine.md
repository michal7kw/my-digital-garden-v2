<h1>L-Theanine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Amino acid found naturally in green tea. Crosses the blood-brain barrier to promote alpha brain wave activity and relaxation without sedation. Modulates glutamate, GABA, serotonin, and dopamine.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 3/5</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Structural analog of glutamate that modulates AMPA and kainate receptors; increases GABA, serotonin, and dopamine levels; promotes alpha wave generation for calm focus.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>100-400mg daily, often combined with caffeine</p>
