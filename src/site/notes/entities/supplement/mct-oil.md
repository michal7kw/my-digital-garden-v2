<h1>MCT Oil</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Medium-chain triglycerides - rapidly absorbed fats that bypass normal digestion and go directly to the liver for ketone production. Supports cognitive function, energy, and ketogenic diets.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)</p>
<p><strong>Research Summary:</strong> Strong evidence for ketone production and cognitive benefits. C8 (caprylic acid) is most ketogenic. Supports ketogenic and intermittent fasting protocols. May benefit Alzheimer's and other neurological conditions through ketone provision.</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>MCTs (6-12 carbon chains) are absorbed directly into portal circulation, bypassing lymphatic system. Liver rapidly converts to ketones (beta-hydroxybutyrate, acetoacetate). Ketones provide alternative brain fuel, may enhance mitochondrial function, and promote satiety.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>5-15ml (1-3 tbsp) daily, starting low</p>
