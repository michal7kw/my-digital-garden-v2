<h1>Bacopa Monnieri</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Ayurvedic herb (Brahmi) with a 3000-year history of use for cognitive enhancement. Active compounds (bacosides A and B) enhance synaptic communication, increase dendritic branching, and upregulate serotonin and acetylcholine.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> 4/5</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Bacosides enhance serotonin and acetylcholine activity; increase dendritic branching in hippocampus; antioxidant protection via SOD and catalase upregulation; modulates GABA and glutamate balance.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>300-600mg daily (standardized to 50% bacosides), effects take 8-12 weeks</p>
