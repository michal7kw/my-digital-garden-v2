<h1>Vitamin C</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Essential water-soluble vitamin and powerful antioxidant. Supports immune function, collagen synthesis, iron absorption, and stress response. Humans cannot synthesize it and must obtain it from diet or supplements.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Electron donor for enzymatic reactions, cofactor for collagen synthesis, regenerates vitamin E, supports adrenal hormone production, enhances iron absorption.</p>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<p>500-2000 mg daily; higher during illness</p>
