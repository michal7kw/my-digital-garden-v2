<h1>viral_antibodies</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The Viral Antibodies Panel is a 14-biomarker panel that screens for latent and active infections from clinically significant viruses: Epstein-Barr virus (EBV), cytomegalovirus (CMV), human herpesvirus 6 (HHV-6), and hepatitis A, B, and C. These viruses can establish lifelong latent infections that reactivate during periods of immune suppression, stress, or aging.</p>
<p>EBV (present in ~95% of adults) is linked to chronic fatigue, certain lymphomas, and autoimmune conditions including multiple sclerosis. CMV and HHV-6 reactivation can cause significant morbidity in immunocompromised patients. The hepatitis panel assesses both current infection status and immune protection (vaccine-induced or natural immunity), with hepatitis C RNA quantification for active viral load assessment. Understanding viral antibody profiles guides immune support strategies and antiviral treatment decisions.</p>
<h2>Included Tests</h2>
<ul>
<li>Epstein-Barr Virus VCA Antibody (IgM) -- Active/recent EBV infection</li>
<li>Epstein-Barr Virus VCA Antibody (IgG) -- Past EBV infection</li>
<li>Epstein-Barr Virus Nuclear Antigen Antibody (IgG) -- Late EBV marker (past infection)</li>
<li>Epstein-Barr Virus Early Antigen D Antibody (IgG) -- EBV reactivation marker</li>
<li>Cytomegalovirus Antibodies (IgM, IgG) -- Active and past CMV infection</li>
<li>Herpesvirus 6 Antibodies (IgM, IgG) -- Active and past HHV-6 infection</li>
<li>Hepatitis A Antibody, Total -- HAV immunity status</li>
<li>Hepatitis B Surface Antigen -- Active hepatitis B infection screening</li>
<li>Hepatitis B Surface Antibody -- Hepatitis B immunity (vaccine or natural)</li>
<li>Hepatitis B Core Antibody, Total -- Past or chronic hepatitis B exposure</li>
<li>Hepatitis C Antibody -- Hepatitis C exposure screening</li>
<li>Hepatitis C Virus RNA, Quantitative PCR -- Active hepatitis C viral load</li>
</ul>
<h2>Clinical Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li>Chronic fatigue syndrome evaluation (EBV/CMV/HHV-6 reactivation)</li>
<li>Pre-transplant or immunosuppression viral risk assessment</li>
<li>Hepatitis screening and immunity verification</li>
<li>Unexplained liver enzyme elevations</li>
<li>Autoimmune disease workup (EBV association with MS, lupus)</li>
</ul>
