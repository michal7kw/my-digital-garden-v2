<h1>gut_health_comprehensive_panel</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The Gut Health Comprehensive Panel combines intestinal inflammation markers, permeability assessment, and microbiome analysis to provide a thorough evaluation of gastrointestinal health. Fecal calprotectin is a reliable non-invasive marker that distinguishes inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) from irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), potentially avoiding unnecessary colonoscopies.</p>
<p>Zonulin measures intestinal permeability (&quot;leaky gut&quot;), a condition increasingly recognized as contributing to systemic inflammation, autoimmunity, and food sensitivities. The GI-MAP (Gastrointestinal Microbial Assay Plus) uses quantitative PCR to detect pathogens, parasites, opportunistic organisms, and beneficial bacteria, providing a comprehensive microbiome profile. This panel is recommended as a first-line workup for unexplained GI symptoms and chronic inflammatory conditions.</p>

<h2>Clinical Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li>Chronic diarrhea, bloating, or abdominal pain of unclear cause</li>
<li>Differentiating IBD from IBS without invasive procedures</li>
<li>Suspected intestinal permeability (leaky gut syndrome)</li>
<li>Post-infectious IBS evaluation</li>
<li>Monitoring response to gut-healing protocols or probiotics</li>
</ul>
