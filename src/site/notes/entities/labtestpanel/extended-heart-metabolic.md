<h1>extended_heart_metabolic</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>This comprehensive set of tests provides a deeper understanding of cardiovascular and metabolic health by evaluating how effectively cholesterol is working, how the body manages blood sugar (including insulin resistance), levels of harmful oxidative stress that can damage cells kidney function, and risks related to blood clotting. These tests are rarely available but trusted and used by top cardiologists. With 98 million Americans at risk of diabetes (prediabetes) and 6 in 10 adults affected by heart disease, understanding your risk is essential for prevention. Strategies like reducing inflammation, improving gut health, and supporting the body’s natural detoxification processes can promote both heart and metabolic health. Included biomarkers (21) Cystatin C-based Estimated Glomerular Filtration Rate (eGFR) AALP Apolipoprotein A1 Apolipoprotein B (ApoB) AALP Apolipoprotein C4 HDL Function pCEC AALP Apolipoprotein C3 AALP Apolipoprotein C1 AALP Apolipoprotein C2 HDL Function pCAD Score Trimethylamine N-Oxide Myeloperoxidase (MPO) Lp-PLA2 Activity Insulin Resistance Score Fibrinogen Antigen, Nephelometry Apolipoprotein B/Apolipoprotein A1 Ratio Apolipoprotein A1 Insulin, Intact</p>
