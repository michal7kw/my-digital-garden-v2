<h1>extended_autoimmunity</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Detects and monitors autoimmune conditions with comprehensive testing for better symptom management and prevention. These tests screen for a broad range of conditions like rheumatoid arthritis, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, Sjögren's syndrome, systemic sclerosis, polymyositis/dermatomyositis, and CREST syndrome. Fifty million Americans have one or more autoimmune diseases, making detection important for managing symptoms and preventing complications. If autoimmunity is detected, anti-inflammatory foods, gut health support, stress management, and targeted supplements can help reduce inflammation and balance immune responses.
