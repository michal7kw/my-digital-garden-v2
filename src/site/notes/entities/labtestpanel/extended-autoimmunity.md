<h1>extended_autoimmunity</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The Extended Autoimmunity Panel is a comprehensive 29-biomarker panel designed to screen for and differentiate among a wide range of autoimmune conditions. The panel covers the major autoimmune disease categories: connective tissue diseases (SLE, systemic sclerosis, mixed connective tissue disease), inflammatory arthritis (rheumatoid arthritis), exocrinopathies (Sjogren's syndrome), antiphospholipid syndrome, and autoimmune thyroiditis.</p>
<p>The panel combines ANA screening and titering with disease-specific antibodies (anti-dsDNA for lupus, anti-CCP for RA, anti-Scl-70 for scleroderma, SS-A/SS-B for Sjogren's), complement levels (C3, C4), inflammatory markers (ESR), and antiphospholipid antibodies. This comprehensive approach enables not only detection of autoimmunity but also differentiation between conditions, which is critical since many autoimmune diseases share overlapping symptoms.</p>

<h2>Clinical Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li>Multi-system symptoms suggestive of autoimmune disease (joint pain, rash, fatigue)</li>
<li>Differentiation between overlapping autoimmune syndromes</li>
<li>Family history of autoimmune conditions</li>
<li>Unexplained positive ANA requiring further characterization</li>
<li>Monitoring disease activity in known autoimmune conditions</li>
</ul>
