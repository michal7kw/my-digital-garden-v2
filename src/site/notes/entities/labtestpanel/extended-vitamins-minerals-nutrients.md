<h1>extended_vitamins_minerals_nutrients</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The Extended Vitamins, Minerals, and Nutrients Panel evaluates key micronutrient and antioxidant levels that are not typically included in standard blood work. The panel measures fat-soluble vitamins (A, E in both alpha and beta-gamma tocopherol forms), Coenzyme Q10, and additional markers critical for energy production, antioxidant defense, immune function, and cellular health.</p>
<p>Subclinical nutrient deficiencies are remarkably common and can contribute to fatigue, cognitive decline, impaired immunity, poor wound healing, and accelerated aging. This panel extends beyond basic vitamin D and B12 testing to capture a more complete micronutrient profile, enabling targeted dietary optimization and evidence-based supplementation strategies.</p>
<h2>Included Tests</h2>
<ul>
<li>Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol) -- Primary antioxidant form of vitamin E</li>
<li>Vitamin E (Beta-Gamma Tocopherol) -- Additional vitamin E isoforms with anti-inflammatory properties</li>
<li>Vitamin A (Retinol) -- Essential for vision, immune function, and skin health</li>
<li>Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10) -- Mitochondrial energy production and antioxidant cofactor</li>
</ul>
<h2>Clinical Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li>Chronic fatigue or unexplained low energy</li>
<li>Suspected micronutrient deficiencies from restrictive diets</li>
<li>Optimization of supplementation regimens</li>
<li>Patients on statins (CoQ10 depletion assessment)</li>
<li>Antioxidant status evaluation in oxidative stress conditions</li>
</ul>
