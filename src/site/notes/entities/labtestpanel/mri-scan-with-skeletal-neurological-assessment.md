<h1>mri_scan_with_skeletal_neurological_assessment</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The MRI Scan with Skeletal and Neurological Assessment is the most comprehensive MRI package, combining full-body, spine, brain, musculoskeletal, and body composition imaging. Beyond the standard MRI and spine coverage, this scan adds dedicated neurodegenerative assessment, cerebrovascular evaluation (head and neck arterial narrowing), and AI-derived brain age analysis to detect premature neurological aging.</p>
<p>The skeletal component includes detailed hip evaluation (labral tears, cartilage damage, joint health) and knee scans (meniscus, ACL, MCL injuries), making this panel valuable for athletes and active individuals. Body composition assessment quantifies visceral fat and skeletal muscle volume, providing metabolic risk information beyond BMI. This is the most thorough single-session imaging assessment available.</p>
<h2>Included Tests</h2>
<ul>
<li>Full-Body MRI -- All organs from standard MRI Scan</li>
<li>Spine MRI -- Cervical, thoracic, and lumbar spine assessment</li>
<li>Brain Neurodegeneration Scan -- Volumetric brain analysis and atrophy assessment</li>
<li>Brain Age Analysis -- AI-derived neurological age vs chronological age</li>
<li>Head/Neck Arterial Assessment -- Carotid and vertebral artery stenosis screening</li>
<li>Body Composition (DEXA-equivalent) -- Fat and muscle volume quantification</li>
<li>Hip MRI -- Labrum, cartilage, and joint evaluation</li>
<li>Knee MRI -- Meniscus, ACL, MCL, and cartilage assessment</li>
</ul>
<h2>Clinical Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li>Comprehensive proactive health screening with neurological and musculoskeletal focus</li>
<li>Athletes or active individuals requiring joint and spine evaluation</li>
<li>Cognitive concerns or family history of neurodegenerative disease</li>
<li>Body composition optimization for metabolic health</li>
<li>Post-injury musculoskeletal assessment combined with whole-body screening</li>
</ul>
