<h1>sexual_health_panel</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The Sexual Health Panel is a comprehensive 13-biomarker STI screening panel that tests for the most prevalent sexually transmitted infections. The panel covers viral infections (HIV, Herpes Simplex 1 and 2), bacterial infections (syphilis via RPR and Treponema pallidum antibodies), and parasitic infections (trichomoniasis with gender-specific testing). HIV testing includes both antigen-antibody combination testing and RNA PCR for early detection during the acute window period.</p>
<p>Many STIs are asymptomatic and can be transmitted unknowingly, making routine screening essential for sexually active individuals. Untreated STIs can lead to serious complications including pelvic inflammatory disease, infertility, increased HIV transmission risk, congenital infections, and certain cancers (cervical cancer from HPV, though HPV is not included in this panel). Early detection enables timely treatment, partner notification, and prevention of long-term complications.</p>
<h2>Included Tests</h2>
<ul>
<li>HIV 1 &amp; 2 Antigen-Antibody -- Combined 4th-generation HIV screening</li>
<li>HIV 1 RNA, QL Real Time PCR -- Acute HIV detection during window period</li>
<li>Herpes Simplex Virus 1 (IgG) -- HSV-1 (oral herpes) antibody detection</li>
<li>Herpes Simplex Virus 2 (IgG) -- HSV-2 (genital herpes) antibody detection</li>
<li>HSV-2 IgG Inhibition, Immunoassay -- Confirmatory HSV-2 testing</li>
<li>Treponema Pallidum AB -- Syphilis-specific antibody testing</li>
<li>Syphilis RPR -- Active syphilis screening and treatment monitoring</li>
<li>Syphilis RPR Titer -- Quantitative syphilis activity assessment</li>
<li>Trichomoniasis (Male and Female) -- Gender-specific parasitic STI testing</li>
</ul>
<h2>Clinical Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li>Routine STI screening for sexually active individuals</li>
<li>New sexual partner evaluation</li>
<li>Symptoms suggestive of STI (discharge, lesions, pain)</li>
<li>Pregnancy screening (HIV, syphilis, hepatitis B are standard prenatal tests)</li>
<li>Post-exposure assessment following high-risk sexual contact</li>
</ul>
