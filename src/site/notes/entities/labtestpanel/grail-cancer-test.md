<h1>grail_cancer_test</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The GRAIL Galleri Multi-Cancer Early Detection (MCED) test is a blood-based liquid biopsy that screens for signals associated with more than 50 types of cancer from a single blood draw. The test analyzes cell-free DNA (cfDNA) methylation patterns to detect cancer-associated signals and predict the tissue of origin, even for cancers that lack standard screening methods.</p>
<p>The Galleri test can detect cancers with existing screening recommendations (breast, lung, colorectal, cervical, prostate) as well as many that currently have no recommended screening, including pancreatic, ovarian, liver, esophageal, and head and neck cancers. The test was validated in adults aged 50 and older with elevated cancer risk. It is designed as a complement to -- not a replacement for -- standard cancer screening. A negative result does not rule out cancer, and a positive result requires confirmatory diagnostic workup.</p>
<h2>Included Tests</h2>
<ul>
<li>Multi-Cancer Signal Detection -- cfDNA methylation pattern analysis for 50+ cancer types</li>
<li>Cancer Signal Origin (CSO) -- Predicted tissue of origin for detected cancer signals</li>
</ul>
<h2>Clinical Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li>Cancer screening in adults aged 50 and older</li>
<li>Individuals at elevated cancer risk (family history, environmental exposures)</li>
<li>Screening for cancers without established standard screening methods</li>
<li>Complement to existing cancer screening protocols (mammography, colonoscopy, etc.)</li>
<li>Proactive health optimization and longevity-focused screening</li>
</ul>
