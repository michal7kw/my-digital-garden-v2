<h1>mthfr_gene</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>This test identifies DNA mutations in the MTHFR (methylenetetrahydrofolate reductase) gene, which influence homocysteine regulation and the metabolism of folate (vitamin B9) and other B vitamins. Both are integral for metabolic and cellular functions. MTHFR mutations are linked to increased risks of cardiovascular disease, mental health issues, dementia, immune dysfunction, and certain cancers. If certain MTHFR gene mutations are present, supplementing with methylated B vitamins (e.g., 5-methylfolate) can help lower homocysteine levels and reduce cardiovascular risk. Other ways to help include detoxification and improving gut health with probiotics, prebiotics, and fiber. Included biomarkers (1)</p>
