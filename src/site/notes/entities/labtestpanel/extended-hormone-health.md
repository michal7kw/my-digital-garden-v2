<h1>extended_hormone_health</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The Extended Hormone Health Panel goes beyond standard reproductive hormone testing to evaluate growth factors, androgen metabolites, and metabolic hormones that influence physical performance, body composition, aging, and recovery. This panel is particularly valuable for individuals experiencing symptoms that standard hormone panels do not fully explain.</p>
<p>IGF-1 reflects growth hormone activity and is relevant to muscle maintenance, bone density, and aging. DHT (dihydrotestosterone) is the most potent androgen and plays a role in hair loss, prostate health, and androgen-sensitive conditions. Androstenedione is a precursor to both testosterone and estrogen. Note: hormonal birth control must be discontinued before testing, as exogenous hormones will confound results.</p>
<h2>Included Tests</h2>
<ul>
<li>Insulin-like Growth Factor (IGF-1) -- Reflects growth hormone activity and anabolic status</li>
<li>Dihydrotestosterone (DHT) -- Potent androgen metabolite affecting hair, skin, and prostate</li>
<li>Androstenedione -- Adrenal/gonadal precursor to testosterone and estrogen</li>
<li>Z Score (Female) -- Age-adjusted hormonal reference scoring</li>
</ul>
<h2>Clinical Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li>Hair loss or hirsutism evaluation</li>
<li>Suspected growth hormone deficiency or excess</li>
<li>Androgen excess syndromes (PCOS evaluation support)</li>
<li>Age-related hormone decline and anti-aging assessment</li>
<li>Athletic performance optimization and recovery monitoring</li>
</ul>
