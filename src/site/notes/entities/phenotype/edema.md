<h1>Edema</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Abnormal accumulation of fluid in tissues causing swelling.</p>
<h2>üß™ Associated Biomarkers</h2>
<p>The following biomarkers should be evaluated when investigating edema:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/garden/dev_admin/labtest/albumin/" class="internal-link">Albumin</a> - Low albumin causes fluid to leak from blood vessels into tissues</li>
<li><a href="/garden/dev_admin/labtest/creatinine/" class="internal-link">Creatinine</a> - Elevated creatinine indicates kidney dysfunction which can cause edema</li>
</ul>
