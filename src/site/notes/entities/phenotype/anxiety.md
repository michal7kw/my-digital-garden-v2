<h1>Anxiety</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>A feeling of apprehension, fear, nervousness, or dread often accompanied by physical symptoms.</p>
<h2>üß™ Associated Biomarkers</h2>
<p>The following biomarkers should be evaluated when investigating anxiety:</p>
<ul>
<li>[[Cortisol]] - Elevated cortisol is associated with chronic stress and anxiety</li>
<li>[[Tsh]] - Thyroid dysfunction (especially hyperthyroidism) can cause anxiety symptoms</li>
<li>[[Vitamin B12]] - B12 deficiency can cause neuropsychiatric symptoms including anxiety</li>
</ul>
