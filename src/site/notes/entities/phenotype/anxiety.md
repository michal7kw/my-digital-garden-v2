# Anxiety

## Description

A feeling of apprehension, fear, nervousness, or dread often accompanied by physical symptoms.

## üß™ Associated Biomarkers

The following biomarkers should be evaluated when investigating anxiety:

- [[Cortisol]] - Elevated cortisol is associated with chronic stress and anxiety
- [[Tsh]] - Thyroid dysfunction (especially hyperthyroidism) can cause anxiety symptoms
- [[Vitamin B12]] - B12 deficiency can cause neuropsychiatric symptoms including anxiety
