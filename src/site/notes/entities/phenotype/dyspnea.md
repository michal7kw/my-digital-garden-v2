<h1>Dyspnea</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath.</p>

<p>The following biomarkers should be evaluated when investigating dyspnea:</p>
<ul>
<li>[[Hemoglobin]] - Low hemoglobin (anemia) reduces oxygen-carrying capacity causing shortness of breath</li>
