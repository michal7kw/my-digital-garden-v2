# Fatigue

## Description

A subjective feeling of tiredness or lack of energy that is not proportional to recent activity level.

## üß™ Associated Biomarkers

The following biomarkers should be evaluated when investigating fatigue:

- [[Hemoglobin]] - Low hemoglobin (anemia) is a primary cause of fatigue
- [[Ferritin]] - Low ferritin indicates iron deficiency, a common cause of fatigue
- [[Tsh]] - Abnormal TSH indicates thyroid dysfunction causing fatigue
- [[Vitamin B12]] - B12 deficiency causes fatigue and neurological symptoms
- [[Vitamin D]] - Low vitamin D is associated with fatigue and weakness
