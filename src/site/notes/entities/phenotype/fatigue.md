<h1>Fatigue</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>A subjective feeling of tiredness or lack of energy that is not proportional to recent activity level.</p>
<h2>üß™ Associated Biomarkers</h2>
<p>The following biomarkers should be evaluated when investigating fatigue:</p>
<ul>
<li>[[Hemoglobin]] - Low hemoglobin (anemia) is a primary cause of fatigue</li>
<li>[[Ferritin]] - Low ferritin indicates iron deficiency, a common cause of fatigue</li>
<li>[[Tsh]] - Abnormal TSH indicates thyroid dysfunction causing fatigue</li>
<li>[[Vitamin B12]] - B12 deficiency causes fatigue and neurological symptoms</li>
<li>[[Vitamin D]] - Low vitamin D is associated with fatigue and weakness</li>
</ul>
