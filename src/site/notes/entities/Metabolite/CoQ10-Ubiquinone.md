# CoQ10-Ubiquinone

## Overview
Coenzyme Q10 (ubiquinone/ubiquinol) is essential for mitochondrial ATP production as part of the electron transport chain. Also functions as a powerful lipid-soluble antioxidant protecting cell membranes and lipoproteins.
