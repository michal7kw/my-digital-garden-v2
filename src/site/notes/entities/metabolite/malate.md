<h1>Malate</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Four-carbon dicarboxylic acid intermediate of the citric acid cycle. Formed by hydration of fumarate and oxidized to oxaloacetate to complete the cycle. Also participates in the malate-aspartate shuttle that transfers reducing equivalents across the mitochondrial membrane.</p>
