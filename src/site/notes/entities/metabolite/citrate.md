<h1>Citrate</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Six-carbon tricarboxylic acid that is the first intermediate of the citric acid cycle. Formed by condensation of acetyl-CoA with oxaloacetate, citrate also serves as a key regulator of glycolysis and fatty acid synthesis.</p>
