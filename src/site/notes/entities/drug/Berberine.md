# Berberine

## Overview
Natural alkaloid compound found in several plants including goldenseal and barberry. Demonstrates significant blood glucose-lowering effects comparable to metformin through AMPK activation. Used traditionally in Chinese and Ayurvedic medicine.

## Pharmacological Information

**Synonyms**:
- Berberine HCl
- Berberine Hydrochloride
- Goldenseal extract
- Huang Lian
