# Amlodipine

## Overview
Long-acting dihydropyridine calcium channel blocker for hypertension and angina. Relaxes vascular smooth muscle, reducing blood pressure. One of the most prescribed antihypertensives.

**Short Description**: Calcium channel blocker for hypertension and angina

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00381

**Drug Class**: Dihydropyridine Calcium Channel Blocker

**Synonyms**:
- Norvasc
- Amvaz

### Mechanism of Action
Blocks L-type calcium channels, causing vascular smooth muscle relaxation
