<h1>Fluvoxamine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Fluvoxamine (Luvox/Faverin) was the first SSRI approved by the FDA specifically for obsessive-compulsive disorder (1994). While it is FDA-approved only for OCD in the US, it is widely used for depression in Europe and Asia. Fluvoxamine has two pharmacological features that distinguish it from all other SSRIs:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Sigma-1 receptor agonism</strong> (Ki 36 nM — highest sigma-1 affinity among SSRIs): The sigma-1 receptor is an ER chaperone protein that modulates BDNF signaling, ER stress responses, and neuroinflammation. This may provide neuroprotective effects beyond standard serotonergic action. Recent interest in fluvoxamine's sigma-1 activity emerged during COVID-19 research (sigma-1 modulation of cytokine storm, PMID:33211890).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Potent CYP1A2 inhibition</strong>: Fluvoxamine is the most potent CYP1A2 inhibitor among SSRIs, increasing levels of theophylline (3x), caffeine (5x), clozapine (5-10x), tizanidine (33x), and other CYP1A2 substrates. This creates critical drug interactions that require careful prescribing.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>SERT inhibition</strong>: Ki 2.2 nM — moderate potency among SSRIs</li>
<li><strong>Sigma-1 receptor agonism</strong>: Unique among SSRIs. May contribute to:
<ul>
<li>BDNF upregulation and neuroprotection</li>
<li>ER stress response modulation</li>
<li>Anti-inflammatory effects (IL-6, TNF-alpha suppression)</li>
<li>Anxiolytic effects independent of serotonin</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>No anticholinergic activity</strong>: Unlike paroxetine</li>
<li><strong>No significant NET/DAT inhibition</strong>: Unlike sertraline</li>
</ul>

<h2>Pharmacogenomics</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>CYP2D6</strong>: Primary metabolic pathway for fluvoxamine itself</li>
<li><strong>CYP inhibition is unrelated to its own metabolism</strong> — fluvoxamine is metabolized by CYP2D6 but inhibits CYP1A2/2C19</li>
<li><strong>Clinical impact</strong>: Patients on fluvoxamine who drink coffee may experience caffeine toxicity (insomnia, anxiety, tremor) due to 5x caffeine level increase</li>
</ul>
