<h1>Penicillin G Benzathine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Penicillin G Benzathine (Bicillin L-A) is a repository penicillin. Following intramuscular injection, it is absorbed very slowly, resulting in low but sustained blood levels for 1 to 4 weeks. It is the gold standard for treating syphilis and for preventing rheumatic fever recurrence.</p>
<h2>Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Syphilis:</strong> Primary, secondary, and latent stages.</li>
<li><strong>Rheumatic Fever Prophylaxis.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Upper Respiratory Infections</strong> (Group A Strep).</li>
</ul>
