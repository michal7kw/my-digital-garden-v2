# MCT8 Oil

## Overview
MCT8 Oil refers to medium-chain triglyceride oil, but the '8' is likely a typo for C8 (caprylic acid), a specific MCT known for its rapid conversion to ketones and potential cognitive benefits. It is a dietary fat derived from coconut or palm kernel oil.
