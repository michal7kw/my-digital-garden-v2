# Acetaminophen

## Overview
Analgesic and antipyretic with minimal anti-inflammatory effect. First-line for mild-moderate pain. Safe at recommended doses but hepatotoxic in overdose. Most common cause of acute liver failure.

**Short Description**: Analgesic and antipyretic for pain and fever

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00316

**Drug Class**: Analgesic / Antipyretic

**Synonyms**:
- Paracetamol
- Tylenol
- APAP

### Mechanism of Action
Central COX inhibition and possible endocannabinoid/serotonergic effects
