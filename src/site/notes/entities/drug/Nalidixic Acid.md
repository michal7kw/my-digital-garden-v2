# Nalidixic Acid

## Overview

Nalidixic Acid was the first quinolone antibiotic introduced. It is effective against most *E. coli*, *Enterobacter*, and *Klebsiella* species but has no activity against *Pseudomonas*. Its use has largely been superseded by newer fluoroquinolones like Ciprofloxacin and Ofloxacin.

## Indications

- **Uncomplicated Urinary Tract Infections.**
