# Rapamycin

## Overview
mTOR inhibitor originally developed as immunosuppressant, now studied for longevity. Inhibits mTOR complex 1 to mimic caloric restriction effects. One of the most promising longevity interventions.

**Short Description**: mTOR inhibitor for immunosuppression and longevity research

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00877

**Drug Class**: mTOR Inhibitor / Immunosuppressant

**Synonyms**:
- Sirolimus
- Rapamune

### Mechanism of Action
Inhibits mTORC1, activating autophagy and mimicking caloric restriction
