# Atorvastatin

## Overview
High-intensity HMG-CoA reductase inhibitor (statin) for cholesterol management. The most prescribed statin worldwide, effective for LDL reduction and cardiovascular risk reduction.

**Short Description**: High-intensity statin for cholesterol reduction and cardiovascular prevention

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB01076

**Drug Class**: HMG-CoA Reductase Inhibitor (Statin)

**Synonyms**:
- Lipitor
- Atorva

### Mechanism of Action
Inhibits HMG-CoA reductase, reduces hepatic cholesterol synthesis, upregulates LDL receptors
