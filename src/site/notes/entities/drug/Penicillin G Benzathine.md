# Penicillin G Benzathine

## Overview

Penicillin G Benzathine (Bicillin L-A) is a repository penicillin. Following intramuscular injection, it is absorbed very slowly, resulting in low but sustained blood levels for 1 to 4 weeks. It is the gold standard for treating syphilis and for preventing rheumatic fever recurrence.

## Indications

- **Syphilis:** Primary, secondary, and latent stages.
- **Rheumatic Fever Prophylaxis.**
- **Upper Respiratory Infections** (Group A Strep).
