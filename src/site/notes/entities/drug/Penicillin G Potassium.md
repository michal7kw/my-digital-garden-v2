# Penicillin G Potassium

## Overview

Penicillin G Potassium is a highly soluble salt form of benzylpenicillin. It is used for the management of severe infections caused by penicillin-sensitive organisms. Due to its potassium content (1.68 mEq/million units), it must be infused slowly when given intravenously to avoid hyperkalemia.

## Indications

- **Severe Pneumonia.**
- **Meningitis.**
- **Septicemia.**
- **Neurosyphilis.**
