# Aspirin

## Overview
Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) with irreversible antiplatelet effects. Used for pain, fever, inflammation, and cardiovascular protection. One of the most widely used medications worldwide.

**Short Description**: NSAID with antiplatelet effects for pain, inflammation, and cardiovascular protection

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00945

**Drug Class**: NSAID / Antiplatelet

**Synonyms**:
- Acetylsalicylic acid
- ASA
- Bayer Aspirin
- Ecotrin

### Mechanism of Action
Irreversibly inhibits COX-1 (platelets) and COX-2 (inflammation)
