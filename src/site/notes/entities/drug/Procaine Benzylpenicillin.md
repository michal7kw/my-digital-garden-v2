# Procaine Benzylpenicillin

## Overview

Procaine Benzylpenicillin (Penicillin G Procaine) is an intramuscular injectable antibiotic. The addition of procaine creates a repository effect, allowing the drug to be released slowly over 24 hours. It is used for moderately severe infections where sustained blood levels are needed.

## Indications

- **Syphilis.**
- **Anthrax.**
- **Diphtheria Carrier State.**
- **Uncomplicated Pneumonia.**
