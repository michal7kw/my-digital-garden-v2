# Prednisone

## Overview
Synthetic corticosteroid with potent anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressive effects. Used for a wide range of inflammatory and autoimmune conditions. Converted to active prednisolone in the liver.

**Short Description**: Corticosteroid for inflammation and immune suppression

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00635

**Drug Class**: Corticosteroid

**Synonyms**:
- Deltasone
- Rayos

### Mechanism of Action
Glucocorticoid receptor activation, suppressing inflammatory gene transcription
