# Losartan

## Overview
Angiotensin II receptor blocker (ARB) for hypertension and diabetic nephropathy. Blocks AT1 receptors, providing similar benefits to ACE inhibitors without the cough. First ARB developed.

**Short Description**: ARB for hypertension and kidney protection

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00678

**Drug Class**: Angiotensin II Receptor Blocker (ARB)

**Synonyms**:
- Cozaar

### Mechanism of Action
Blocks AT1 receptors, preventing angiotensin II effects
