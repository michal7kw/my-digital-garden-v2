# Piracetam

## Overview
The original nootropic drug, a cyclic derivative of GABA. Enhances membrane fluidity and modulates neurotransmission. Used in Europe for cognitive disorders; not approved in US.

**Short Description**: Original nootropic for cognitive enhancement

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB09210

**Drug Class**: Nootropic (Racetam)

**Synonyms**:
- Nootropil
- Lucetam
- Breinox

### Mechanism of Action
Enhances membrane fluidity, modulates AMPA receptors, improves cellular metabolism
