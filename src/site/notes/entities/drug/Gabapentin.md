# Gabapentin

## Overview
Anticonvulsant and analgesic used for neuropathic pain, epilepsy, and restless leg syndrome. Binds to alpha-2-delta calcium channel subunit. Not actually a GABA analog despite the name.

**Short Description**: Gabapentinoid for neuropathic pain and epilepsy

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00996

**Drug Class**: Gabapentinoid / Anticonvulsant

**Synonyms**:
- Neurontin
- Gralise

### Mechanism of Action
Binds alpha-2-delta calcium channel subunit, modulating neurotransmitter release
