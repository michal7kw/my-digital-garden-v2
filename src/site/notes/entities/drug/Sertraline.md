# Sertraline

## Overview
Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI) antidepressant. First-line treatment for depression, anxiety disorders, OCD, and PTSD. Well-studied with favorable safety profile.

**Short Description**: SSRI antidepressant for depression, anxiety, OCD, and PTSD

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB01104

**Drug Class**: Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitor (SSRI)

**Synonyms**:
- Zoloft
- Lustral

### Mechanism of Action
Selectively blocks serotonin reuptake, increasing synaptic 5-HT
