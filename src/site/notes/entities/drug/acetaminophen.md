<h1>Acetaminophen</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Analgesic and antipyretic with minimal anti-inflammatory effect. First-line for mild-moderate pain. Safe at recommended doses but hepatotoxic in overdose. Most common cause of acute liver failure.</p>
