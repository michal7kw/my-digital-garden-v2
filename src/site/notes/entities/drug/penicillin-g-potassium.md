<h1>Penicillin G Potassium</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Penicillin G Potassium is a highly soluble salt form of benzylpenicillin. It is used for the management of severe infections caused by penicillin-sensitive organisms. Due to its potassium content (1.68 mEq/million units), it must be infused slowly when given intravenously to avoid hyperkalemia.</p>
<h2>Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Severe Pneumonia.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Meningitis.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Septicemia.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Neurosyphilis.</strong></li>
</ul>
