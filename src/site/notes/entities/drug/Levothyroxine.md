# Levothyroxine

## Overview
Synthetic thyroid hormone (T4) for hypothyroidism replacement therapy. The most prescribed medication for thyroid disorders. Requires careful dose titration based on TSH levels.

**Short Description**: Synthetic T4 for hypothyroidism treatment

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00451

**Drug Class**: Thyroid Hormone

**Synonyms**:
- Synthroid
- Levoxyl
- Tirosint
- L-thyroxine

### Mechanism of Action
Replaces endogenous T4; converted to active T3 in peripheral tissues
