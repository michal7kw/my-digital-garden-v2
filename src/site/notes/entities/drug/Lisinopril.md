# Lisinopril

## Overview
ACE inhibitor for hypertension, heart failure, and diabetic nephropathy. Reduces angiotensin II formation, lowering blood pressure and providing cardio-renal protection.

**Short Description**: ACE inhibitor for blood pressure and cardio-renal protection

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00722

**Drug Class**: ACE Inhibitor

**Synonyms**:
- Prinivil
- Zestril

### Mechanism of Action
Inhibits ACE, reducing angiotensin II and aldosterone, causing vasodilation
