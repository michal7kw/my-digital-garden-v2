# Ashwagandha

## Overview
Ashwagandha is a revered herb in traditional Indian medicine, classified as an adaptogen due to its remarkable capacity to help the body adapt to various stressors, both physical and psychological. It is increasingly popular in modern wellness for its multifaceted health benefits.
