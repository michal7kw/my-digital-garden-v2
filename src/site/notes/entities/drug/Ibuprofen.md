# Ibuprofen

## Overview
Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) for pain, fever, and inflammation. Reversibly inhibits COX-1 and COX-2 enzymes. One of the most widely used OTC pain relievers.

**Short Description**: NSAID for pain, inflammation, and fever

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB01050

**Drug Class**: NSAID

**Synonyms**:
- Advil
- Motrin
- Nurofen

### Mechanism of Action
Reversibly inhibits COX-1 and COX-2, reducing prostaglandins
