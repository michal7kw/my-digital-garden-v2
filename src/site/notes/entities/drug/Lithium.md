# Lithium

## Overview
Mood stabilizer and gold standard for bipolar disorder. Also studied at micro-doses for neuroprotection and longevity. Modulates multiple signaling pathways including GSK-3β and inositol.

**Short Description**: Mood stabilizer for bipolar disorder with neuroprotective effects

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB01356

**Drug Class**: Mood Stabilizer

**Synonyms**:
- Lithium carbonate
- Lithobid
- Eskalith

### Mechanism of Action
Inhibits GSK-3β, depletes inositol, promotes BDNF and neuroprotection
