# Ibuprofen Sodium

## Overview

Ibuprofen Sodium is a fast-acting formulation of the NSAID ibuprofen. By using the sodium salt, the drug dissolves more quickly in the stomach, leading to faster blood levels and quicker relief of acute pain compared to standard ibuprofen acid tablets.

## Indications

- **Acute Pain Relief.**
- **Headache/Migraine.**
- **Dental Pain.**
- **Fever.**
