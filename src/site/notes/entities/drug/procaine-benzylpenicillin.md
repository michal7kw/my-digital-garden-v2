<h1>Procaine Benzylpenicillin</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Procaine Benzylpenicillin (Penicillin G Procaine) is an intramuscular injectable antibiotic. The addition of procaine creates a repository effect, allowing the drug to be released slowly over 24 hours. It is used for moderately severe infections where sustained blood levels are needed.</p>
<h2>Indications</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Syphilis.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Anthrax.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Diphtheria Carrier State.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Uncomplicated Pneumonia.</strong></li>
</ul>
