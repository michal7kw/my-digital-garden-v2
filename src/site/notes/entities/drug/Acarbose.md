# Acarbose

## Overview
Alpha-glucosidase inhibitor that slows carbohydrate absorption. Reduces postprandial glucose spikes. Studied for longevity due to caloric restriction mimetic properties.

**Short Description**: Alpha-glucosidase inhibitor for postprandial glucose control

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00284

**Drug Class**: Alpha-Glucosidase Inhibitor

**Synonyms**:
- Precose
- Glucobay

### Mechanism of Action
Inhibits intestinal alpha-glucosidase, slowing carbohydrate absorption
