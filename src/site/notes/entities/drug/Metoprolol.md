# Metoprolol

## Overview
Cardioselective beta-1 blocker for hypertension, angina, heart failure, and arrhythmias. Reduces heart rate and myocardial oxygen demand. One of the most prescribed cardiovascular medications.

**Short Description**: Cardioselective beta blocker for hypertension and heart conditions

## Pharmacological Information

**DrugBank ID**: DB00264

**Drug Class**: Beta-1 Selective Blocker

**Synonyms**:
- Lopressor
- Toprol-XL

### Mechanism of Action
Selective beta-1 blockade, reducing heart rate and myocardial oxygen demand
