<h1>Dopamine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Monoamine neurotransmitter central to reward, motivation, motor control, and executive function. Often called the &quot;motivation molecule,&quot; it drives goal-directed behavior and is implicated in addiction, Parkinson's disease, and ADHD.</p>
