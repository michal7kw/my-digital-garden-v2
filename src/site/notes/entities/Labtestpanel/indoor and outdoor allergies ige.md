# indoor_and_outdoor_allergies_ige

These tests help identify specific indoor and outdoor allergens that trigger allergic reactions or exacerbate underlying respiratory conditions like asthma. Identifying environmental allergens can help guide interventions to minimize symptoms, reduce the risk of severe allergic complications, and significantly improve quality of life. The test is important for preventing complications from environmental allergies like asthma, allergic rhinitis, conjunctivitis, atopic dermatitis, and urticaria. *Allergens tested may vary by region.
