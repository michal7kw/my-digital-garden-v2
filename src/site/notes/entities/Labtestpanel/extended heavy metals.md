# extended_heavy_metals

Heavy metals can accumulate in the body over time, often without causing noticeable symptoms. However, elevated levels of these metals can contribute to serious health problems, including brain, heart, and kidney issues. Testing for heavy metals helps to identify exposure and guide detoxification. Arsenic is commonly found in contaminated water, rice, seafood, and crops grown in arsenic-rich soil or irrigated with arsenic-contaminated water.
