# mri_scan

Scans for a broad spectrum of conditions, including cancers of the brain, thyroid, liver, gallbladder, pancreas, spleen, kidneys, adrenal glands, bladder, ovaries, uterus, and prostate. Also scans for signs of stroke, sinus inflammation, fatty liver, gallstones, uterine fibroids, kidney stones, and abdominal aortic aneurysm.
