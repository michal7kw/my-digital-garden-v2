# extended_immune_regulation

These tests help evaluate susceptibility to infections, detect potential immunodeficiency disorders, and identify autoimmune conditions, providing essential insights for maintaining immune system balance and overall health. Immunoglobulins are antibodies that play a vital role in the immune system, defending against bacterial, viral, and other infections. Included biomarkers (7) Immunoglobulin G (IgG) Immunoglobulin A (IgA) Immunoglobulin M (IgM)
