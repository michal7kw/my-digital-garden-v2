# mri_scan_with_skeletal_neurological_assessment

Everything in the MRI Scan with Spine, plus scans for signs of neurodegeneration in the brain and narrowing in the arteries of the head and neck, and provides a brain age analysis. Also includes body composition assessment (fat and muscle volume), hip evaluation (labrum, cartilage, and joint health), and knee scans (for meniscus, ACL, and MCL injuries).
