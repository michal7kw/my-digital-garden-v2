<h1>Low Glycemic Index Diet</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Dietary pattern emphasizing foods with a glycemic index below 55, promoting stable blood sugar and improved insulin sensitivity.<br />
<strong>Type</strong>: diet</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Low-GI foods produce a slower, more gradual rise in blood glucose, reducing insulin demand, promoting satiety, and lowering postprandial lipemia. Fiber and resistant starch slow gastric emptying.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>may not suit high-performance athletes needing rapid glycogen replenishment</li>
<li>individuals with gastroparesis (high fiber may worsen symptoms)</li>
</ul>
