<h1>Elimination Diet</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Systematic diagnostic diet that removes common allergenic and inflammatory foods for 2-4 weeks, then reintroduces them one at a time to identify triggers. Gold standard for food sensitivity identification.<br />
<strong>Type</strong>: diet</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Removes potential immune triggers (IgE and non-IgE-mediated), allowing intestinal inflammation to resolve. Systematic reintroduction identifies specific foods that provoke symptoms via immune activation, histamine release, or enzyme deficiency.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>eating disorder history</li>
<li>severe malnutrition</li>
<li>should be guided by healthcare professional</li>
<li>not appropriate for known IgE-mediated allergies (use strict avoidance instead)</li>
</ul>
