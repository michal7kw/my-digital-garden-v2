<h1>Cold Exposure</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Deliberate exposure to cold temperatures (e.g., ice baths, cold showers) to trigger hormetic stress responses and metabolic benefits.</p>
<p><strong>Type</strong>: lifestyle</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Activates the sympathetic nervous system, leading to rapid release of norepinephrine and activation of brown adipose tissue (BAT) for thermogenesis.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h3>Dosing Information</h3>
<p>{'protocol_name': 'The Huberman 11-Minute Protocol'}</p>
<h3>Administration</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Frequency</strong>: 2-4 times per week</li>
<li><strong>Duration</strong>: 1-5 minutes per session (totaling 11 mins/week)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Side Effects</h3>
<ul>
<li>Cold shock response</li>
<li>Potential hypothermia if overdone</li>
</ul>
