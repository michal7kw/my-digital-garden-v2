<h1>Low-FODMAP Diet</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Therapeutic elimination diet restricting fermentable oligosaccharides, disaccharides, monosaccharides, and polyols. Clinically validated for IBS symptom management. Consists of elimination (2-6 weeks), reintroduction, and personalization phases.<br />
<strong>Type</strong>: diet</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Reduces osmotic water draw into the intestinal lumen and limits substrate for rapid bacterial fermentation, decreasing gas production, bloating, and visceral hypersensitivity. Modulates gut microbiome composition toward lower gas-producing species.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>eating disorder history</li>
<li>underweight individuals</li>
<li>should not be followed long-term without reintroduction</li>
<li>children without dietitian supervision</li>
</ul>
