<h1>Mitochondrial Uncoupling</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>A metabolic intervention where the proton gradient in mitochondria is dissipated (leaked) without generating ATP, forcing the cell to burn more fuel to maintain the potential. Mimics exercise and activates thermogenesis.</p>
<p><strong>Type</strong>: metabolic_therapy</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Induces proton leak across the inner mitochondrial membrane, activating AMPK and increasing fatty acid oxidation.</p>
