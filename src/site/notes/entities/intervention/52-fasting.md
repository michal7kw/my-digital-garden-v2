<h1>5:2 Fasting</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Modified fasting protocol: eat normally 5 days per week, restrict to 500-600 calories on 2 non-consecutive days. Provides intermittent energy restriction without daily time restriction.<br />
<strong>Type</strong>: diet</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Two weekly caloric restriction days activate AMPK, reduce IGF-1, improve insulin sensitivity, and promote autophagy without sustained caloric deficit. Metabolic switching between fed and fasted states improves metabolic flexibility.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>type 1 diabetes</li>
<li>eating disorder history</li>
<li>pregnancy/breastfeeding</li>
<li>children and adolescents</li>
<li>underweight individuals</li>
</ul>
