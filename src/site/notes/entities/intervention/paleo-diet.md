<h1>Paleo Diet</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Ancestral eating pattern emphasizing whole foods available to Paleolithic humans: meat, fish, vegetables, fruits, nuts, and seeds. Excludes grains, legumes, dairy, refined sugar, and processed foods.<br />
<strong>Type</strong>: diet</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Eliminates processed foods and refined carbohydrates, reducing glycemic load and inflammatory triggers. High protein increases thermogenesis and satiety. Omega-3-rich food selection improves omega-6:omega-3 ratio. Elimination of lectins and gluten may reduce intestinal permeability.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>chronic kidney disease (high protein)</li>
<li>calcium deficiency risk without dairy</li>
<li>athletes needing high glycogen availability</li>
<li>disordered eating tendencies</li>
</ul>
