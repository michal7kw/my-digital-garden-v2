<h1>Plant-Based/Vegan Diet</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Diet excluding all animal products, relying entirely on plant foods. Rich in fiber, polyphenols, and phytochemicals. Requires careful planning to avoid nutrient deficiencies (B12, iron, zinc, omega-3).<br />
<strong>Type</strong>: diet</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>High fiber intake promotes beneficial gut microbiome composition and SCFA production. Abundant polyphenols reduce oxidative stress. Low saturated fat intake lowers LDL. Phytosterols compete with cholesterol absorption. Low caloric density supports weight management.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>B12, iron, zinc, or omega-3 deficiency if unsupplemented</li>
<li>pregnancy/breastfeeding without professional guidance</li>
<li>children without careful nutritional planning</li>
<li>anemia</li>
</ul>
