<h1>Carnivore Diet</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>All-animal-product diet eliminating all plant foods. Based on the premise that plant antinutrients and fiber may cause digestive issues in sensitive individuals. Highly restrictive and controversial.<br />
<strong>Type</strong>: diet</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Zero carbohydrate intake induces sustained ketosis. Elimination of plant antinutrients (lectins, oxalates, phytates) may reduce GI inflammation in susceptible individuals. High protein intake supports satiety and muscle protein synthesis. Bioavailable heme iron and B12.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>familial hypercholesterolemia</li>
<li>chronic kidney disease</li>
<li>gout history</li>
<li>colon cancer history</li>
<li>individuals with TMAO concerns</li>
<li>pregnancy without medical supervision</li>
</ul>
