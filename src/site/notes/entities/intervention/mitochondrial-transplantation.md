<h1>Mitochondrial Transplantation</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The transfer of healthy, functional mitochondria into cells or tissues with damaged mitochondria. A cutting-edge &quot;Mitotherapy&quot; for aging and ischemic injury.</p>
<p><strong>Type</strong>: cellular_therapy</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Direct replacement of dysfunctional organelles with healthy ones (exogenous or autologous), restoring bioenergetics.</p>
