<h1>Intermittent Fasting 16:8</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Time-restricted eating with a 16-hour fasting window and 8-hour eating window. Promotes autophagy, insulin sensitivity, and metabolic flexibility.<br />
<strong>Type</strong>: diet</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Extended fasting lowers insulin, activates AMPK and autophagy, shifts fuel utilization toward fatty acid oxidation, and improves circadian rhythm alignment.</p>

<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>type 1 diabetes</li>
<li>eating disorder history</li>
<li>pregnancy/breastfeeding</li>
<li>underweight individuals</li>
<li>children and adolescents</li>
</ul>
