<h1>Zone 2 Training</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Moderate-intensity aerobic exercise performed at a pace that allows for steady-state fat oxidation and mitochondrial development.</p>
<p><strong>Type</strong>: exercise</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Stimulates mitochondrial biogenesis and enhances fat oxidation capacity by staying below the first lactate threshold (LT1).</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h3>Dosing Information</h3>
<p>{'protocol_name': 'Conversational Pace Cardio'}</p>
<h3>Administration</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Timing</strong>: Anytime, but often used as a foundation for athletic training.</li>
<li><strong>Frequency</strong>: 3-4 sessions per week</li>
<li><strong>Duration</strong>: 45-90 minutes per session</li>
</ul>
