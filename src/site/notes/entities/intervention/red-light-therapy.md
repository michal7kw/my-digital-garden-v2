<h1>Red Light Therapy</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Red light therapy (RLT) is a therapeutic technique that uses red low-level wavelengths of light to treat skin issues, sleep problems, and chronic pain, among other things.</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Penetrates skin to stimulate mitochondria, promoting ATP production, reducing inflammation, and enhancing cellular repair.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>Photosensitivity</li>
<li>Active skin conditions (e.g., severe eczema)</li>
<li>Pregnancy (consult physician)</li>
</ul>
<h3>Side Effects</h3>
<ul>
<li>Mild redness or irritation (rare)</li>
<li>Eye strain (if unprotected)</li>
</ul>
