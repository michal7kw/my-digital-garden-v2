<h1>DASH Diet</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension — emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and low-fat dairy while restricting sodium and saturated fat.<br />
<strong>Type</strong>: diet</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>High potassium, magnesium, and calcium intake promotes vasodilation and natriuresis. Low sodium reduces fluid retention. Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<h2>Safety Information</h2>
<h3>Contraindications</h3>
<ul>
<li>chronic kidney disease (high potassium)</li>
<li>severe sodium restriction with diuretic use</li>
<li>potassium-sparing medication interactions</li>
</ul>
