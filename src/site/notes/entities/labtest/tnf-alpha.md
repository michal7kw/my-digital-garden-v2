<h1>Tumor Necrosis Factor Alpha (TNF-α)</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Tumor Necrosis Factor Alpha (TNF-alpha) test measures this key pro-inflammatory cytokine in blood. TNF-alpha is produced primarily by activated macrophages and plays a central role in systemic inflammation. It is a therapeutic target in rheumatoid arthritis, inflammatory bowel disease, and psoriasis.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
