# HDL Cholesterol

## Overview
Tested twice a year Think of the H in HDL as healthy, helpful, heart-supporting cholesterol. HDL's primary job is to remove excess cholesterol from the blood and transport it to the liver to be eliminated, reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)

## Test Information

|----------|-------|
| **Category** | Blood Panel |
| **Subcategory** | Lipid Panel || **Test Type** | Standard |
| **Sample Type** | Blood |
| **Collection Method** | Venipuncture |
| **Turnaround Time** | 3-7 days || **Price Range** | $null-$null || **HSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes || **FSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes |
## Fasting Requirements

⚠️ **Fasting Required**

- Fast for **12 hours** before the test
- Water is generally permitted during fasting
- Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise by your provider
