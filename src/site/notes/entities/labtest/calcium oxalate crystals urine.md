# calcium_oxalate_crystals_urine

## Overview
Tested twice a year Calcium oxalate crystals can occasionally appear in urine without causing harm, often reflecting diet or hydration status. However, persistent crystal formation can be associated with kidney stones, chronic kidney problems, and underlying metabolic imbalances.
