# lymphocytes

## Overview
Tested twice a year Lymphocytes are specialized white blood cells (WBCs) consisting of T cells, B cells, and natural killer (NK) cells, each orchestrating a different arm of immune defense. Their counts can rise or fall with various conditions, providing clues about immune health. The test includes both percentage and absolute levels.
