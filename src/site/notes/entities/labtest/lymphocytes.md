<h1>Lymphocytes</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Tested twice a year Lymphocytes are specialized white blood cells (WBCs) consisting of T cells, B cells, and natural killer (NK) cells, each orchestrating a different arm of immune defense. Their counts can rise or fall with various conditions, providing clues about immune health. The test includes both percentage and absolute levels.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
