# bacteria_urine

## Overview
Tested twice a year Testing for bacteria in urine can help identify urinary tract infections (UTIs), by detecting microbes that should not normally be present in urine. Finding bacteria in urine can also uncover contamination from improper sample collection, making it an useful tool for diagnosis and guiding treatment.
