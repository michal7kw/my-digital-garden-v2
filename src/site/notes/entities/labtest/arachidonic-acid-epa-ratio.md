<h1>Arachidonic Acid/EPA Ratio</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>uh-rak-ih-don-ik as-id / e-p-a ray-shee-oh The AA/EPA ratio measures the balance between arachidonic acid (AA), a pro-inflammatory omega-6 fatty acid, and eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA), an anti-inflammatory omega-3 fatty acid. A higher ratio means the body may be more prone to fueling inflammation rather than resolving it, making this an important marker for understanding the body's inflammatory environment.</p>
