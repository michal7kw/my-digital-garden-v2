<h1>Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP)</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>14-test panel measuring glucose, electrolytes, kidney function (BUN, creatinine), liver enzymes (ALT, AST, ALP), and proteins.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)</p>

<h2>Fasting Requirements</h2>
<p>⚠️ <strong>Fasting Required</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Fast for <strong>10 hours</strong> before the test</li>
<li>Water is generally permitted during fasting</li>
<li>Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise by your provider</li>
</ul>
<h2>Preparation Instructions</h2>
<ol>
<li>Fast for 10-12 hours</li>
<li>Water is permitted</li>
<li>Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise</li>
</ol>
