# Eosinophils

## Overview
Tested twice a year Eosinophils are a type of white blood cell that defend against parasitic worms or helminths, regulate immune responses, and contribute to allergic and inflammatory conditions. They release enzymes and inflammatory molecules to attack pathogens, but can also damage tissues if overactive. The test includes both percentage and absolute levels.
