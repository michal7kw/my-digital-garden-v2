<h1>Thyroid Peroxidase Antibodies (TPO)</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>TPO antibodies are proteins made by the immune system that target an enzyme needed for thyroid hormone production. Detecting TPO antibodies can reveal autoimmune activity that may impair thyroid function. Possible symptoms UNEXPLAINED WEIGHT GAIN OR WEIGHT LOSS COLD INTOLERANCE CONSTIPATION HAIR THINNING OR HAIR LOSS</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
