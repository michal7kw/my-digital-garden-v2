# Leukocyte, Urine

## Overview
Tested twice a year Leukocytes (white blood cells) are part of the immune system and normally help the body fight infection. When they appear in urine, which is called leukocyturia, it can signal inflammation in the urinary tract.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
