# DUTCH Complete (Hormones)

## Overview
Dried urine test for comprehensive hormone analysis including cortisol patterns, sex hormones, and metabolites.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)

## Test Information

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Category** | Functional Medicine |
| **Subcategory** | Dutch Hormone || **Test Type** | Standard |
| **Sample Type** | Blood |
| **Collection Method** | Venipuncture |
| **Turnaround Time** | 14-21 days || **Price Range** | $399-$499 || **HSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes || **FSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes |

## Preparation Instructions

1. Collect 4-5 samples over 24 hours
2. Avoid certain supplements
3. Time collection around menstrual cycle for women



## Recommended For

### Supplement Monitoring
No specific supplement monitoring recommendations.

### Conditions
General health screening.
