# rheumatoid_factor_rf

## Overview
Rheumatoid factor (RF) is an antibody commonly associated with autoimmune conditions like rheumatoid arthritis, rheumatoid vasculitis, or Sjögren’s syndrome. Higher levels of RF typically point to more severe complications of the autoimmune condition like joint damage.
