<h1>Protein, Urine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Tested twice a year Protein is normally filtered and reabsorbed by the kidneys, so healthy urine contains little to no detectable protein. When protein appears in higher amounts, a condition known as proteinuria, it can be a sign of kidney stress or injury.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
