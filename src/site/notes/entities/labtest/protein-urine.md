# Protein, Urine

## Overview
Tested twice a year Protein is normally filtered and reabsorbed by the kidneys, so healthy urine contains little to no detectable protein. When protein appears in higher amounts, a condition known as proteinuria, it can be a sign of kidney stress or injury.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
