<h1>Partial Thromboplastin Time PTT</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time (aPTT) test. Evaluates the intrinsic and common coagulation pathways (Factors XII, XI, IX, VIII, X, V, II, I). Used for monitoring unfractionated heparin therapy and screening for coagulation disorders.</p>
