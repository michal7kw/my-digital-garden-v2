<h1>Non-HDL Cholesterol</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Tested twice a year Non-HDL cholesterol is the total amount of harmful cholesterol in the blood, contributing to overall cardiovascular risk. Elevated levels are commonly seen in individuals with high triglycerides, low HDL, and underlying insulin resistance or diabetes.</p>
