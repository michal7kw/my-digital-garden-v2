# triiodothyronine_t3_free

## Overview
try-eye-oh-doh-thigh-ruh-neen tee-three free Triiodothyronine (T3) Free is the active thyroid hormone that directly regulates metabolism, energy levels, and body temperature. Most T3 is made by converting T4 into this active form. Measuring free T3 helps show how well this conversion is working and how much active thyroid hormone is available for the body to use.
