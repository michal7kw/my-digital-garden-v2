# Basic Metabolic Panel (BMP)

## Overview
8-test panel measuring glucose, calcium, electrolytes, and kidney function markers.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

## Test Information

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Category** | Blood Panel |
| **Subcategory** | Bmp || **Test Type** | Standard |
| **Sample Type** | Blood |
| **Collection Method** | Venipuncture |
| **Turnaround Time** | 1-2 days || **Price Range** | $20-$45 || **HSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes || **FSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes |
## Fasting Requirements

⚠️ **Fasting Required**

- Fast for **8 hours** before the test
- Water is generally permitted during fasting
- Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise by your provider
