# Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP)

## Overview
14-test panel measuring glucose, electrolytes, kidney function (BUN, creatinine), liver enzymes (ALT, AST, ALP), and proteins.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

| **Turnaround Time** | 1-3 days || **Price Range** | $25-$65 || **HSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes || **FSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes |
## Fasting Requirements

⚠️ **Fasting Required**

- Fast for **10 hours** before the test
- Water is generally permitted during fasting
- Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise by your provider

## Preparation Instructions

1. Fast for 10-12 hours
2. Water is permitted
3. Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise
