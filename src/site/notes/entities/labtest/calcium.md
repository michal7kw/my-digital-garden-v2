# calcium

## Overview
Tested twice a year Calcium levels help understand symptoms related to the kidneys, bones, thyroid, parathyroid, or nerves. Calcium is a mineral the body uses to build and maintain strong bones, teeth, muscle control, and blood circulation. Possible symptoms FATIGUE OR WEAKNESS MUSCLE CRAMPS, TWITCHING, OR SPASMS ABDOMINAL PAIN, NAUSEA, OR CONSTIPATION BONE OR JOINT PAIN CONFUSION OR BRAIN FOG
