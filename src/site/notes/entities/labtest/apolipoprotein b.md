# Apolipoprotein B (ApoB)

## Overview
Measures ApoB protein, present in all atherogenic lipoprotein particles. Considered superior to LDL-C for CVD risk.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)
