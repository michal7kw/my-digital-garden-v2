<h1>Red Blood Cell Count</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>red blud sell kownt Tested twice a year Red blood cell (RBC) count is a key marker of health that reflects how well the body can carry oxygen to tissues, which is essential for energy and overall function. Abnormal RBC levels—too low or too high—can be linked to various conditions, poor quality of life, and increased oxidative stress, making it an important window into both wellness and disease.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
