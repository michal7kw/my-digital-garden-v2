# hdl_cholesterol

## Overview
Tested twice a year Think of the H in HDL as healthy, helpful, heart-supporting cholesterol. HDL's primary job is to remove excess cholesterol from the blood and transport it to the liver to be eliminated, reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease.
