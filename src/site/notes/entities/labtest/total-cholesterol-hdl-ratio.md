<h1>Total Cholesterol / HDL Ratio</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>toh-tul koh-les-ter-ol / h-d-l ray-shee-oh Tested twice a year The ratio shows the balance between harmful cholesterol particles that cause plaque buildup in arteries, and protective cholesterol particles that help remove excess cholesterol from the blood. A higher ratio is typically associated with a higher risk of future cardiovascular events like heart attack and stroke.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
