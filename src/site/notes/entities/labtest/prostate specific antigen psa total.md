# prostate_specific_antigen_psa_total

## Overview
pros-tate speh-sif-ik an-tih-jen (p-s-a) toh-tul Total prostate-specific antigen (PSA) measures the overall amount of PSA in the blood, including both PSA that is attached to proteins and PSA that is free. This test is considered alongside free PSA and used to calculate percent-free PSA.
