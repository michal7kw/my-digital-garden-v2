# Iron Panel (Complete)

## Overview
Measures serum iron, ferritin, TIBC, transferrin saturation. Essential for anemia workup and iron overload assessment.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

## Test Information

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Category** | Blood Panel |
| **Subcategory** | Iron Panel || **Test Type** | Standard |
| **Sample Type** | Blood |
| **Collection Method** | Venipuncture |
| **Turnaround Time** | 1-3 days || **Price Range** | $35-$85 || **HSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes || **FSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes |
## Fasting Requirements

⚠️ **Fasting Required**

- Fast for **12 hours** before the test
- Water is generally permitted during fasting
- Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise by your provider

## Preparation Instructions

1. Fast for 12 hours
2. Morning sample preferred
3. Avoid iron supplements for 24 hours
