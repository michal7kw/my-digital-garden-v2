<h1>Lipid Panel (Standard)</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Measures total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, and triglycerides. Essential for cardiovascular risk assessment.</p>

<h2>Fasting Requirements</h2>
<p>⚠️ <strong>Fasting Required</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Fast for <strong>12 hours</strong> before the test</li>
<li>Water is generally permitted during fasting</li>
<li>Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise by your provider</li>
</ul>
<h2>Preparation Instructions</h2>
<ol>
<li>Fast for 9-12 hours</li>
<li>Avoid alcohol for 24 hours</li>
<li>Continue regular medications</li>
</ol>
