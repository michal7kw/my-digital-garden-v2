<h1>LDH</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Lactate Dehydrogenase (LDH) test. Non-specific tissue damage marker present in nearly all body tissues. Elevated in hemolytic anemia, liver disease, lymphoma, and myocardial infarction. Also a prognostic marker in oncology.</p>
