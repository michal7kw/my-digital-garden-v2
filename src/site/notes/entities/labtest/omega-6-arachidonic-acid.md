<h1>Omega-6: Arachidonic Acid</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Arachidonic acid is an omega-6 fatty acid that helps regulate inflammation and immune function. Because excessive AA can promote chronic inflammatory activity, measuring it provides important insight into risks for conditions like heart disease, diabetes, and arthritis.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
