<h1>Procalcitonin</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Procalcitonin (PCT) test. Serum biomarker that rises specifically in response to bacterial infections. Used to guide initiation and discontinuation of antibiotic therapy, particularly in sepsis and lower respiratory tract infections.</p>
