# Albumin (Microalbumin - Urine)

## Overview
Albumin in the urine, even in small amounts—a condition called microalbuminuria—is one of the earliest warning signs that the kidneys’ filters are under stress and may be starting to leak protein they normally keep in the blood.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
