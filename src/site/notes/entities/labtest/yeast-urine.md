<h1>Yeast, Urine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Tested twice a year Yeast in the urine, most often Candida species, may sometimes reflect contamination from the skin or genital area, but persistent presence often signals a fungal urinary tract infection (UTI) known as candiduria.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
