# Yeast, Urine

## Overview
Tested twice a year Yeast in the urine, most often Candida species, may sometimes reflect contamination from the skin or genital area, but persistent presence often signals a fungal urinary tract infection (UTI) known as candiduria.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
