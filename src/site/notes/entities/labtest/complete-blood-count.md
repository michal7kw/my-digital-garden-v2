# Complete Blood Count (CBC)

## Overview
Measures red blood cells, white blood cells, hemoglobin, hematocrit, and platelets. Essential for detecting anemia, infection, and blood disorders.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

## Test Information

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Category** | Blood Panel |
| **Subcategory** | Cbc || **Test Type** | Standard |
| **Sample Type** | Blood |
| **Collection Method** | Venipuncture |
| **Turnaround Time** | 1-2 days || **Price Range** | $15-$45 || **HSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes || **FSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes |
