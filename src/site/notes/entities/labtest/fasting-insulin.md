# Fasting Insulin

## Overview
Measures insulin levels after fasting. Key marker for insulin resistance and metabolic health.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

## Test Information

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Category** | Hormones |
| **Subcategory** | Metabolic Hormones || **Test Type** | Standard |
| **Sample Type** | Blood |
| **Collection Method** | Venipuncture |
| **Turnaround Time** | 1-3 days || **Price Range** | $25-$55 || **HSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes || **FSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes |
## Fasting Requirements

⚠️ **Fasting Required**

- Fast for **12 hours** before the test
- Water is generally permitted during fasting
- Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise by your provider
