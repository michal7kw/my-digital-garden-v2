# GI-MAP (GI Microbial Assay Plus)

## Overview
Comprehensive stool DNA test for gut microbiome, pathogens, parasites, and digestive markers.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)

## Preparation Instructions

1. Stop probiotics 2 weeks before
2. Avoid laxatives
3. Collect first morning sample
