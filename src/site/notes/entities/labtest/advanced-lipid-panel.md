# Advanced Lipid Panel (NMR)

## Overview
NMR spectroscopy-based panel providing LDL particle number, size, HDL particles, and lipoprotein(a). Superior cardiovascular risk stratification.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

## Test Information

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Category** | Blood Panel |
| **Subcategory** | Lipid Panel || **Test Type** | Standard |
| **Sample Type** | Blood |
| **Collection Method** | Venipuncture |
| **Turnaround Time** | 3-7 days || **Price Range** | $75-$200 || **HSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes || **FSA Eligible** | ✅ Yes |
## Fasting Requirements

⚠️ **Fasting Required**

- Fast for **12 hours** before the test
- Water is generally permitted during fasting
- Take medications as usual unless advised otherwise by your provider


## Biomarkers Measured

This test measures the following biomarkers:

- [[ldl_p]]
- [[ldl_size]]
- [[hdl_p]]
- [[lp_a]]
- [[apoB]]


## Recommended For

### Supplement Monitoring
No specific supplement monitoring recommendations.

### Conditions
General health screening.

## Reference Ranges

Reference ranges vary by laboratory and testing method. Consult your healthcare provider for interpretation.
