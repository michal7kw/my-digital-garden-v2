<h1>C-Terminal Telopeptide (CTX / beta-CrossLaps)</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>C-Terminal Telopeptide (CTX or beta-CrossLaps) measures a collagen degradation fragment released during bone resorption. It is the most widely used marker of bone resorption and is recommended by the IOF/IFCC as the reference resorption marker. Used to monitor anti-resorptive therapy in osteoporosis.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
