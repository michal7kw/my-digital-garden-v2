<h1>HDL Large</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>HDL particles can be small or large. Larger, “fluffier” HDL particles are more protective because they are better at removing excess cholesterol from the arteries, helping reduce the risk of atherosclerosis and heart disease.</p>
