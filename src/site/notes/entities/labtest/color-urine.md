# Color, Urine

## Overview
Tested twice a year Evaluates the pigmentation of urine, which can range from pale yellow to dark amber. Variations in color may be influenced by hydration status, dietary intake, medication use, or various underlying medical conditions.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
