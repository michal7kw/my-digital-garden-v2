# mean_corpuscular_hemoglobin_mch

## Overview
meen kor-pus-kyoo-lur hee-muh-gloh-bin (m-c-h) Tested twice a year Mean corpuscular hemoglobin (MCH) measures the average amount of hemoglobin, the oxygen-carrying protein, in each red blood cell. Low MCH often points to iron deficiency anemia, while high MCH can signal a vitamin B12 or folate deficiency.
