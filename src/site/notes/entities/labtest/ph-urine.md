<h1>pH, Urine</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Tested twice a year Urine pH reflects how acidic or alkaline the urine is, and it plays a key role in helping the body maintain overall acid–base balance. Abnormal urine pH can increase the risk of kidney stones, or indicate metabolic or dietary imbalances that may warrant medical attention.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
