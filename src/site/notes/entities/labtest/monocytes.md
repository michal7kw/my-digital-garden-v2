# Monocytes

## Overview
Tested twice a year Monocytes are immune cells important for defense, inflammation regulation, and tissue repair. These white blood cells can transform into macrophages or dendritic cells to fight infections, clear debris, and promote healing. The test includes both percentage and absolute levels.

**Evidence Level:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
