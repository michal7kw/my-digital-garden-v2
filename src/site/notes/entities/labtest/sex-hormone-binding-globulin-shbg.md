<h1>Sex Hormone Binding Globulin (SHBG)</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Sex hormone binding globulin (SHBG) is a protein made by the liver that regulates how much estrogen and testosterone are available for the body to use. In females, SHBG helps maintain hormone balance and can influence fertility, menstrual cycles, and symptoms related to conditions like PCOS or excess hair growth (hirsutism). Measuring SHBG provides useful clues about hormone health, particularly when there are signs of excess androgens, irregular cycles, or unexplained symptoms.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
