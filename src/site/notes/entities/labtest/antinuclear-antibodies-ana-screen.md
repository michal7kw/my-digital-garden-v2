# Antinuclear Antibodies (ANA) Screen

## Overview
an-tee-noo-klee-ur an-tih-bod-eez skreen Antinuclear antibodies (ANA) screen looks for antibodies that sometimes target the body’s own cells. A positive result may suggest autoimmune activity, but it is also found in many healthy people. Because of this, ANA results are interpreted alongside other tests and symptoms to determine whether further evaluation is needed. Possible symptoms FATIGUE OR MALAISE JOINT PAIN OR ARTHRITIS SKIN RASHES RAYNAUD’S PHENOMENON (COLOR CHANGES IN FINGERS OR TOES WITH COLD OR STRESS) HAIR LOSS (ALOPECIA)
