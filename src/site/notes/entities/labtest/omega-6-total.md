<h1>Omega-6 Total</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Omega-6 Total measures the key omega-6 fatty acids that make up a large portion of the body’s essential fat stores. While omega-6s are important building blocks for cells and play a role in growth, energy, and normal immune function, excess levels can promote inflammation. This marker provides insight into the body’s overall fatty acid balance and inflammatory environment..</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
