<h1>Glaucoma</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>A group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, often caused by abnormally high pressure in the eye, leading to progressive vision loss.</p>
<h2>Symptoms</h2>
<ul>
<li>Open-angle: Usually asymptomatic; gradual peripheral vision loss</li>
<li>Angle-closure: Acute eye pain, headache, halos around lights -- emergency</li>
</ul>
<h2>Treatment Approaches</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>First-line</strong>: Prostaglandin analogs (latanoprost) -- reduce IOP 25-35%</li>
<li><strong>Laser</strong>: SLT -- now first-line option per LiGHT trial</li>
<li><strong>Surgery</strong>: Trabeculectomy, MIGS for advanced disease</li>
<li><strong>Supplements</strong>: Ginkgo biloba (ocular blood flow), omega-3, vitamin C</li>
</ul>
