# Tobacco Use Disorder

## Overview

Tobacco Use Disorder involves the addiction to nicotine found in tobacco products. Nicotine is highly addictive, increasing **dopamine** in the brain's reward circuits. It is a chronic, relapsing condition and is the leading cause of preventable death worldwide.

## Complications

- **Cancer:** Lung, mouth, throat, bladder.
- **Respiratory:** COPD, Emphysema, Chronic Bronchitis.
- **Cardiovascular:** Heart attack, Stroke, PAD.
