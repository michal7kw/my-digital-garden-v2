# Hemorrhage

## Overview

Hemorrhage (bleeding) is the loss of blood from the circulatory system. It can range from minor (a small cut) to life-threatening (massive internal bleeding).

## Classification

- **External:** Visible bleeding from skin wounds.
- **Internal:** Bleeding into body cavities (e.g., Intracranial, Gastrointestinal).
- **Arterial:** Bright red, spurting (high pressure).
- **Venous:** Dark red, steady flow.
- **Capillary:** Oozing.
