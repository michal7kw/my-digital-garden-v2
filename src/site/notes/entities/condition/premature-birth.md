<h1>Premature Birth</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Premature (Preterm) Birth is the leading cause of death in children under 5 worldwide. Babies born early may have more health problems than babies born on time, and may face long-term health issues that affect the brain, lungs, hearing, or vision.</p>
<h2>Classifications</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Extremely Preterm:</strong> &lt;28 weeks.</li>
<li><strong>Very Preterm:</strong> 28 to 32 weeks.</li>
<li><strong>Moderate to Late Preterm:</strong> 32 to 37 weeks.</li>
</ul>
