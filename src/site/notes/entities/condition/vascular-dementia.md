<h1>Vascular Dementia</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Cognitive impairment caused by cerebrovascular disease, including strokes and small vessel disease. The second most common cause of dementia after Alzheimer's disease, often occurring in combination (mixed dementia).</p>
