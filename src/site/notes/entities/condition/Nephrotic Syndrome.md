# Nephrotic Syndrome

## Overview

Nephrotic Syndrome results from damage to the **Glomeruli** (kidney filters). It is defined by a triad of:
1.  **Massive Proteinuria** (>3.5g/day).
2.  **Hypoalbuminemia** (low blood protein).
3.  **Edema**.
It also commonly involves **Hyperlipidemia** (high blood fats).

## Causes

- **Primary:** Minimal Change Disease (common in children), FSGS, Membranous Nephropathy.
- **Secondary:** Diabetic Nephropathy, Lupus Nephritis.
