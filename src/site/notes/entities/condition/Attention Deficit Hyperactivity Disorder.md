# Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder

## Overview

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is one of the most common neurodevelopmental disorders of childhood, often lasting into adulthood. It affects executive function, including the ability to plan, focus, remember instructions, and multitask.

## Types

1.  **Predoministently Inattentive:** Difficulty organizing or finishing a task, paying attention to details, or following instructions.
2.  **Predominantly Hyperactive-Impulsive:** Fidgets, talks a lot, has difficulty sitting still, interrupts others.
3.  **Combined Presentation:** Symptoms of both.
