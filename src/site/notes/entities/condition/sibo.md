<h1>SIBO</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Condition characterized by excessive bacterial colonization of the small intestine, which is normally relatively sterile compared to the colon. Three subtypes based on dominant gas produced — hydrogen-dominant (diarrhea-predominant), methane-dominant/IMO (constipation-predominant), and hydrogen sulfide (mixed/diarrhea). Causes bloating, malabsorption, and systemic inflammation. Often comorbid with IBS, hypothyroidism, and motility disorders.</p>
