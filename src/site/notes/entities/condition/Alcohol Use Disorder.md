# Alcohol Use Disorder

## Overview

Alcohol Use Disorder (AUD) is a medical condition characterized by a problematic pattern of alcohol use. It encompasses conditions often referred to as alcohol abuse, alcohol dependence, and alcoholism. AUD affects the brain's reward, motivation, and memory circuitry.

## Complications

- **Liver Disease:** Fatty liver, hepatitis, cirrhosis.
- **Cardiovascular:** Hypertension, cardiomyopathy.
- **Neurological:** Wernicke-Korsakoff syndrome, dementia.
- **Cancer:** Increased risk of mouth, throat, liver, and colon cancer.
