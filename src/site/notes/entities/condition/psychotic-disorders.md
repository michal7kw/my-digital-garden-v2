<h1>Psychotic Disorders</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Psychotic disorders are severe mental disorders that cause abnormal thinking and perceptions. People with psychoses lose touch with reality. Two of the main symptoms are <strong>delusions</strong> (false beliefs) and <strong>hallucinations</strong> (false perceptions).</p>
<h2>Types</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Schizophrenia:</strong> Chronic condition with delusions, hallucinations, and social withdrawal.</li>
<li><strong>Schizoaffective Disorder:</strong> Symptoms of schizophrenia + mood disorder (depression/bipolar).</li>
<li><strong>Brief Psychotic Disorder:</strong> Short-term psychosis triggered by stress.</li>
<li><strong>Substance-Induced Psychotic Disorder.</strong></li>
</ul>
