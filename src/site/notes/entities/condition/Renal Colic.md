# Renal Colic

## Overview

Renal Colic is the specific type of pain you feel when urinary stones block part of your urinary tract. The pain is caused by the ureter stretching as it tries to move the stone and from urine backing up into the kidney. It is often described as one of the worst pains a person can experience.
