# Premature Birth

## Overview

Premature (Preterm) Birth is the leading cause of death in children under 5 worldwide. Babies born early may have more health problems than babies born on time, and may face long-term health issues that affect the brain, lungs, hearing, or vision.

## Classifications

- **Extremely Preterm:** <28 weeks.
- **Very Preterm:** 28 to 32 weeks.
- **Moderate to Late Preterm:** 32 to 37 weeks.
