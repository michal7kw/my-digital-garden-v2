<h1>Cardiovascular Disease</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Cardiovascular disease (CVD) is the leading cause of death globally (17.9 million deaths/year). It encompasses coronary artery disease, heart failure, stroke, and peripheral arterial disease. The primary driver is atherosclerosis — a chronic inflammatory process where oxidized LDL infiltrates arterial walls, triggering plaque formation over decades. Modern risk assessment includes ApoB (better predictor than LDL-C), Lp(a) (genetic risk in 20% of population), hs-CRP, and coronary artery calcium scoring.</p>
<h2>Symptoms</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Coronary artery disease</strong>: Chest pain (angina), shortness of breath, fatigue</li>
<li><strong>Heart failure</strong>: Dyspnea, peripheral edema, exercise intolerance</li>
<li><strong>Stroke</strong>: Face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty (FAST)</li>
<li>Often asymptomatic until acute event</li>
</ul>
<h2>Risk Factors</h2>
<ul>
<li>Hypertension, dyslipidemia (elevated ApoB/LDL, low HDL, high Lp(a))</li>
<li>Smoking, type 2 diabetes, obesity, physical inactivity</li>
<li>Chronic inflammation (elevated hs-CRP)</li>
<li>Family history, chronic stress, air pollution</li>
</ul>
<h2>Treatment Approaches</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Lifestyle</strong>: Mediterranean diet (PREDIMED — 30% CVD reduction), exercise (150-300 min/week), smoking cessation, weight management</li>
<li><strong>Lipid management</strong>: Statins, ezetimibe, PCSK9 inhibitors, bempedoic acid</li>
<li><strong>Blood pressure</strong>: ACE inhibitors/ARBs, CCBs, thiazides (target &lt;130/80)</li>
<li><strong>Anti-inflammatory</strong>: Colchicine (LoDoCo2 — 31% event reduction)</li>
<li><strong>Antiplatelet</strong>: Low-dose aspirin (secondary prevention)</li>
<li><strong>Supplements</strong>: Omega-3 EPA (REDUCE-IT — 25% risk reduction at 4g/day), CoQ10, magnesium, vitamin K2</li>
</ul>
