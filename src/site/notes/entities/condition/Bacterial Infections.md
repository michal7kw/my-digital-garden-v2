# Bacterial Infections

## Overview

Bacteria are single-celled microorganisms. Most are harmless or beneficial (microbiome), but some are pathogenic and cause disease. Bacterial infections can affect any part of the body. They are distinct from viral infections and are treated with antibiotics.

## Common Types

- **Skin:** Cellulitis, Impetigo, Staph infection.
- **Respiratory:** Pneumonia, Tuberculosis, Strep throat.
- **Gastrointestinal:** Salmonella, E. coli.
- **Urinary:** UTI.
- **Systemic:** Sepsis.
