# Pancreatic Insufficiency

## Overview

Exocrine Pancreatic Insufficiency (EPI) results in the inability to digest fats, proteins, and carbohydrates. This leads to **Malabsorption** and chronic diarrhea. It is a common long-term complication of chronic inflammation of the pancreas.

## Complications

- **Malnutrition.**
- **Osteoporosis:** Due to Vitamin D malabsorption.
- **Sarcopenia:** Due to protein malabsorption.
