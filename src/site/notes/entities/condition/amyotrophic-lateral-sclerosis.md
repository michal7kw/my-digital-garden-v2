<h1>Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>A fatal neurodegenerative disease characterized by progressive loss of upper and lower motor neurons, leading to muscle weakness, paralysis, and death typically within 2-5 years of symptom onset.</p>
