<h1>Autoimmune Hepatitis</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>A chronic liver disease in which the body's immune system mistakenly attacks liver cells, causing inflammation and liver damage.</p>
<h2>Symptoms</h2>
<ul>
<li>Fatigue, jaundice, abdominal discomfort, arthralgia</li>
<li>Elevated transaminases, hypergammaglobulinemia</li>
</ul>
<h2>Treatment Approaches</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>First-line</strong>: Prednisone (induction) + azathioprine (maintenance) -- 80% remission</li>
<li><strong>Second-line</strong>: Mycophenolate mofetil, tacrolimus</li>
<li><strong>Liver transplant</strong>: For decompensated cirrhosis</li>
<li><strong>Supplements</strong>: NAC, vitamin D, probiotics</li>
</ul>
