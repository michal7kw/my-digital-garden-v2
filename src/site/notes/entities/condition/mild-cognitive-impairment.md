<h1>Mild Cognitive Impairment</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>An intermediate stage between normal age-related cognitive decline and dementia. Represents increased risk for progression to Alzheimer's disease or other dementias, but may remain stable or improve.</p>
