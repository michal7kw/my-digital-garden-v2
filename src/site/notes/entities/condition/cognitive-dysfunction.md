<h1>Cognitive Dysfunction</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>A deficit in intellectual functioning, including problems with memory, attention, language, thinking, and judgment.</p>
<h2>Symptoms</h2>
<ul>
<li>Memory loss (especially short-term)</li>
<li>Difficulty concentrating (brain fog)</li>
<li>Confusion and disorientation</li>
<li>Difficulty finding words</li>
<li>Impaired judgment and decision-making</li>
<li>Difficulty with executive function (planning, organizing)</li>
<li>Slowed processing speed</li>
<li>Reduced ability to multitask</li>
</ul>
<h2>Treatment Approaches</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Address underlying causes</strong>: Correct thyroid dysfunction, B12/folate deficiency, sleep disorders, depression, medication side effects</li>
<li><strong>Cognitive training</strong>: Structured brain exercises, memory strategies, attention training, cognitive rehabilitation programs</li>
<li><strong>Exercise</strong>: Aerobic exercise (150 min/week) — strongest evidence for cognitive preservation. Increases BDNF, hippocampal volume, and cerebral blood flow</li>
<li><strong>Supplements</strong>: Omega-3 DHA (1-2 g/day — key structural brain fatty acid), Lion's Mane mushroom (NGF stimulation), phosphatidylserine (supports membrane fluidity), creatine (brain energy metabolism), B-complex vitamins (lower homocysteine)</li>
<li><strong>Diet</strong>: Mediterranean or MIND diet — strong epidemiological evidence for cognitive protection. Rich in polyphenols (blueberries, dark chocolate), leafy greens, nuts, olive oil</li>
<li><strong>Lifestyle</strong>: Sleep optimization (7-9 hours — critical for memory consolidation and glymphatic clearance), stress reduction (chronic cortisol is neurotoxic), social engagement, lifelong learning</li>
<li><strong>Pharmacological</strong>: Cholinesterase inhibitors (donepezil — for dementia), modafinil (for fatigue-related cognitive issues — off-label), address vascular risk factors</li>
</ul>
