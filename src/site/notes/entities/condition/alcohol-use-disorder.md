<h1>Alcohol Use Disorder</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Alcohol Use Disorder (AUD) is a medical condition characterized by a problematic pattern of alcohol use. It encompasses conditions often referred to as alcohol abuse, alcohol dependence, and alcoholism. AUD affects the brain's reward, motivation, and memory circuitry.</p>
<h2>Complications</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Liver Disease:</strong> Fatty liver, hepatitis, cirrhosis.</li>
<li><strong>Cardiovascular:</strong> Hypertension, cardiomyopathy.</li>
<li><strong>Neurological:</strong> Wernicke-Korsakoff syndrome, dementia.</li>
<li><strong>Cancer:</strong> Increased risk of mouth, throat, liver, and colon cancer.</li>
</ul>
