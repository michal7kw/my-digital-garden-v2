<h1>Frontotemporal Dementia</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>A group of neurodegenerative disorders characterized by progressive degeneration of the frontal and temporal lobes. Causes changes in personality, behavior, and language, typically with earlier onset than Alzheimer's disease.</p>
