# Chronic Inflammation

## Overview
Chronic inflammation is a prolonged inflammatory response that can last for weeks, months, or even years. It is characterized by the simultaneous active inflammation, tissue destruction, and attempts at healing.

## Clinical Presentation
### Symptoms
- Fatigue
- Fever
- Rash
- Joint pain
- Muscle stiffness
- Weight gain/loss
