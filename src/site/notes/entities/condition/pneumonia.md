<h1>Pneumonia</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>An infection that inflames the air sacs (alveoli) in one or both lungs, which may fill with fluid or pus.</p>
<h2>Symptoms</h2>
<ul>
<li>Cough (productive), fever, dyspnea, pleuritic chest pain</li>
<li>Tachycardia, crackles on auscultation</li>
<li>Elderly: confusion, falls, no fever (atypical)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Treatment Approaches</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Outpatient</strong>: Amoxicillin, macrolide, or respiratory fluoroquinolone</li>
<li><strong>Inpatient</strong>: Beta-lactam + macrolide</li>
<li><strong>Prevention</strong>: Pneumococcal vaccine, influenza vaccine, COVID vaccine</li>
<li><strong>Supplements</strong>: Vitamin C, vitamin D (reduces risk if deficient), zinc, NAC</li>
</ul>
