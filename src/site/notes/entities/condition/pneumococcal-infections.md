# Pneumococcal Infections

## Overview

Pneumococcal disease is any type of infection caused by *Streptococcus pneumoniae* (pneumococcus). These bacteria can cause many types of illnesses, including pneumonia (infection of the lungs), ear infections, sinus infections, meningitis (infection of the covering around the brain and spinal cord), and bacteremia (bloodstream infection).

## Types

- **Non-Invasive:** Otitis media, Sinusitis.
- **Invasive:** Pneumonia, Bacteremia, Meningitis.
