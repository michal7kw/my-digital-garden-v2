# Polycystic Ovary Syndrome

## Overview

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal imbalance that affects ovulation. The ovaries may develop numerous small collections of fluid (follicles) and fail to regularly release eggs. It is a leading cause of infertility.

## Complications

- **Infertility.**
- **Metabolic Syndrome:** High blood pressure, high blood sugar, abnormal cholesterol.
- **Type 2 Diabetes.**
- **Sleep Apnea.**
- **Endometrial Cancer.**
