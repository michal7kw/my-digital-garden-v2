<h1>MCAS</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Chronic condition characterized by inappropriate and excessive mast cell activation and mediator release affecting multiple organ systems. Distinct from mastocytosis (clonal mast cell proliferation). Mast cells degranulate in response to triggers releasing histamine, prostaglandins, leukotrienes, and other mediators. Causes episodic multisystem symptoms. Diagnosis requires clinical criteria (Vienna consensus) — symptoms, evidence of mediator elevation, and response to mast cell-targeted therapy.</p>
