# Cushings Syndrome

## Overview

Cushing's Syndrome (Hypercortisolism) can be **Exogenous** (caused by medications) or **Endogenous** (caused by the body). **Cushing's Disease** specifically refers to the condition when a pituitary tumor overproduces ACTH. Excess cortisol has devastating effects on metabolism, skin integrity, and bone density.

## Complications

- **Osteoporosis and Fractures.**
- **Type 2 Diabetes.**
- **Hypertension.**
- **Muscle Wasting.**
- **Immune Suppression.**
