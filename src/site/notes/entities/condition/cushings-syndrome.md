<h1>Cushings Syndrome</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Cushing's Syndrome (Hypercortisolism) can be <strong>Exogenous</strong> (caused by medications) or <strong>Endogenous</strong> (caused by the body). <strong>Cushing's Disease</strong> specifically refers to the condition when a pituitary tumor overproduces ACTH. Excess cortisol has devastating effects on metabolism, skin integrity, and bone density.</p>
<h2>Complications</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Osteoporosis and Fractures.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Type 2 Diabetes.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Hypertension.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Muscle Wasting.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Immune Suppression.</strong></li>
</ul>
