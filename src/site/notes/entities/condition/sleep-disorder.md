# Sleep Disorder

## Overview

Sleep disorders impact overall health, mental well-being, and safety. Common disorders include **Insomnia**, **Obstructive Sleep Apnea**, **Narcolepsy**, and **Restless Legs Syndrome**. Chronic poor sleep quality is linked to increased risk of cardiovascular disease and metabolic dysfunction.

## Key Types

- **Insomnia:** Difficulty initiating or maintaining sleep.
- **Sleep Apnea:** Breathing interruptions during sleep.
- **Circadian Rhythm Disorders:** Misalignment of the internal clock.
- **Parasomnias:** Abnormal behaviors during sleep (e.g., sleepwalking).
