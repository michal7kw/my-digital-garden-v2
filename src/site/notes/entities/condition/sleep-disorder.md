<h1>Sleep Disorder</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Sleep disorders impact overall health, mental well-being, and safety. Common disorders include <strong>Insomnia</strong>, <strong>Obstructive Sleep Apnea</strong>, <strong>Narcolepsy</strong>, and <strong>Restless Legs Syndrome</strong>. Chronic poor sleep quality is linked to increased risk of cardiovascular disease and metabolic dysfunction.</p>
<h2>Key Types</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Insomnia:</strong> Difficulty initiating or maintaining sleep.</li>
<li><strong>Sleep Apnea:</strong> Breathing interruptions during sleep.</li>
<li><strong>Circadian Rhythm Disorders:</strong> Misalignment of the internal clock.</li>
<li><strong>Parasomnias:</strong> Abnormal behaviors during sleep (e.g., sleepwalking).</li>
</ul>
