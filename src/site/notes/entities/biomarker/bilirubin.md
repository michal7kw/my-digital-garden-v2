<h1>Bilirubin</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Bilirubin is a yellowish bile pigment formed during the normal breakdown of red blood cells (hemoglobin). It serves as a key indicator of liver function, biliary health, and hemolytic processes.</p>
