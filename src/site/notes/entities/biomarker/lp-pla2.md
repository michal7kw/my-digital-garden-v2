<h1>Lp-PLA2</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Lipoprotein-associated phospholipase A2 is a vascular-specific inflammatory enzyme produced by macrophages within atherosclerotic plaques. Elevated levels indicate active vascular inflammation and unstable plaque, independently predicting coronary events and ischemic stroke.</p>
