<h1>C-Peptide</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>C-Peptide is a 31-amino acid polypeptide cleaved from proinsulin during insulin biosynthesis, serving as a direct measure of endogenous insulin production by pancreatic beta cells.</p>
