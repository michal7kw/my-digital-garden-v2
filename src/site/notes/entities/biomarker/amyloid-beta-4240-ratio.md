<h1>Amyloid-Beta 42/40 Ratio</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The ratio of amyloid-beta 42 to amyloid-beta 40 peptides in CSF or plasma. A decreased ratio indicates amyloid pathology and is a core biomarker for Alzheimer's disease within the ATN framework.</p>
