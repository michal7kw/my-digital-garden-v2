<h1>Apolipoprotein B</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Apolipoprotein B (ApoB) is the primary structural protein found in all atherogenic (plaque-forming) lipoprotein particles, including LDL, VLDL, and IDL. It is considered a superior predictor of cardiovascular risk compared to LDL-C alone.</p>
