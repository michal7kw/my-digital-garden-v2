<h1>CA 125</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Cancer antigen 125 is a glycoprotein tumor marker primarily used for monitoring ovarian cancer treatment response and recurrence. Elevations also occur in endometriosis, pelvic inflammatory disease, and other benign conditions.</p>
<h2>Relationships</h2>
<h3>Indicates Organ Health</h3>
<p>INDICATES_ORGAN_HEALTH::<a class="internal-link is-unresolved" href="/404">Ovaries</a> (primary tumor marker for ovarian cancer monitoring and recurrence detection, Evidence Level 1a)</p>
<h3>Increases Risk For</h3>
<p>INCREASES_RISK_FOR::<a class="internal-link is-unresolved" href="/404">Ovarian Cancer</a> (when elevated, Evidence Level 1a)</p>
