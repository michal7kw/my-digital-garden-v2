<h1>Acute Kidney Injury Markers</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>A group of specialized protein biomarkers (NGAL, KIM-1, Cystatin C, TIMP-2, IGFBP7) used for the early detection, risk stratification, and monitoring of acute kidney injury (AKI), often detecting damage 24-48 hours before serum creatinine rises.</p>
