<h1>Serotonin</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Serum serotonin (5-hydroxytryptamine, 5-HT) measures peripheral serotonin levels. Approximately 95% of serotonin is produced in the gut, with the remainder in the CNS. Serum levels primarily reflect gut-derived serotonin stored in platelets. Clinically used for carcinoid syndrome diagnosis and as a research biomarker for mood disorders. Low platelet serotonin is associated with depression, anxiety, and sleep disturbances.</p>
