<h1>Alanine Transaminase</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Alanine Transaminase (ALT) is an enzyme primarily found in liver cells. It is released into the bloodstream when liver cells are damaged, making it a sensitive indicator of hepatocyte injury.</p>
