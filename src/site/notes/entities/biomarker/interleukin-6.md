<h1>Interleukin 6</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Interleukin-6 is a pleiotropic pro-inflammatory cytokine and key mediator of the acute-phase inflammatory response, produced by T cells, macrophages, and endothelial cells.</p>
