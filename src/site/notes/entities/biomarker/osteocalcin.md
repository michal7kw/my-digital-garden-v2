<h1>Osteocalcin</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Osteocalcin is a non-collagenous protein produced by osteoblasts that serves as a marker of bone formation. It also functions as a hormone affecting glucose metabolism and energy expenditure.</p>
