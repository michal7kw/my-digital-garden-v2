<h1>AA/EPA Ratio</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The AA/EPA ratio measures the balance between arachidonic acid (AA), a pro-inflammatory omega-6 fatty acid, and eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA), an anti-inflammatory omega-3 fatty acid. It is a critical marker of cellular inflammation and cardiovascular risk.</p>
