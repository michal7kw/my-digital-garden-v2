<h1>MPO</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Myeloperoxidase is a heme peroxidase enzyme released by activated neutrophils and monocytes. Elevated levels indicate oxidative stress and vascular inflammation, independently predicting acute coronary events and heart failure outcomes.</p>
