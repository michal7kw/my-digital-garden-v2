<h1>Hormonal Balance</h1>
<p>Support endocrine health with adaptogens and key micronutrients.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Ashwagandha has strong RCT evidence for cortisol reduction (14-28%) and testosterone increase in men. Vitamin D and zinc are well-established for HPG axis function. Selenium and iodine are essential for thyroid hormone synthesis. Magnesium supports HPA axis regulation and sleep quality, indirectly supporting hormonal health.</p>
