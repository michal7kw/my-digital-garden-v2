<h1>Energy &amp; Vitality</h1>
<p>Boost energy with essential vitamins and track key deficiency markers.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>B-vitamin deficiency is a well-documented cause of fatigue. Iron supplementation improves energy in deficient individuals. Vitamin D insufficiency affects 40-50% of adults.</p>
