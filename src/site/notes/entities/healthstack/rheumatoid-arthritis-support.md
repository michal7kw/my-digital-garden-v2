<h1>Rheumatoid Arthritis Support</h1>
<p>Anti-inflammatory and joint-protective supplements for RA management.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Omega-3 fish oil has Level A evidence from multiple RCTs for reducing joint tenderness and NSAID use in RA. Curcumin and vitamin D have Level B evidence. Probiotics and boswellia have Level C evidence from smaller trials.</p>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Evidence-based adjunctive supplement protocol for RA patients on conventional DMARD therapy. This stack focuses on reducing inflammatory cytokines (TNF-alpha, IL-6), modulating NF-kB signaling, and supporting joint integrity through multiple complementary mechanisms. It is NOT a replacement for DMARDs -- adjunctive only.</p>

<h2>Important Notes</h2>
<ul>
<li>This stack is adjunctive to DMARD therapy, NOT a replacement</li>
<li>Discuss all supplements with rheumatologist before starting</li>
<li>Omega-3 may increase bleeding risk with anticoagulants</li>
<li>Curcumin may interact with some medications (CYP3A4)</li>
</ul>
