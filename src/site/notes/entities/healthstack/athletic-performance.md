<h1>Athletic Performance</h1>
<p>Enhance athletic output and recovery with proven performance supplements.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Creatine is the gold standard for strength and power gains (ISSN position stand). Beta-alanine improves performance in 60-240 second efforts. Citrulline malate reduces post-exercise muscle soreness.</p>
