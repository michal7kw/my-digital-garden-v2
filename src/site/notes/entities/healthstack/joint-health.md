<h1>Joint Health</h1>
<p>Protect cartilage, reduce joint inflammation, maintain mobility.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Collagen peptides have moderate-quality evidence for reducing joint pain and improving cartilage markers. Glucosamine/chondroitin evidence is mixed but positive in specific subgroups (Kellgren-Lawrence grade 2-3). Omega-3 and curcumin have strong anti-inflammatory evidence relevant to joint health. Vitamin C is essential for collagen synthesis.</p>
