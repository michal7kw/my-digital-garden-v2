<h1>Sleep Quality</h1>
<p>Enhance sleep quality with calming nutrients and sleep-supporting minerals.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Magnesium glycinate is well-tolerated and enhances sleep quality in RCTs. L-theanine promotes relaxation without sedation. Glycine at 3g before bed improves subjective sleep quality.</p>
