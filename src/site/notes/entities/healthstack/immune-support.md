<h1>Immune Support</h1>
<p>Strengthen immune defenses with essential vitamins and minerals.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Vitamin D supplementation reduces respiratory infection risk by 12% (BMJ meta-analysis). Zinc reduces cold duration by 33%. Elderberry shortens flu duration by 2-4 days in small trials.</p>
