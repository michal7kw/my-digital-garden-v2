<h1>Mood &amp; Stress</h1>
<p>Manage stress and support mood with adaptogens and key nutrients.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Ashwagandha has multiple RCTs showing cortisol reduction. Omega-3 EPA shows antidepressant effects in meta-analyses. Magnesium deficiency is linked to anxiety and depression.</p>
