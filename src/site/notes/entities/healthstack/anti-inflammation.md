<h1>Anti-Inflammation</h1>
<p>Fight chronic inflammation with evidence-backed anti-inflammatory compounds.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Omega-3 and curcumin have extensive evidence for reducing inflammatory markers. Quercetin shows promise as a senolytic compound. Vitamin D modulates over 200 immune-related genes.</p>
