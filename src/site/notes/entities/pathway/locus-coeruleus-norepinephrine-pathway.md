<h1>Locus Coeruleus Norepinephrine Pathway</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Noradrenergic projections from the locus coeruleus to virtually all brain regions. Controls arousal, attention, and the stress response. Tonic firing maintains wakefulness; phasic firing enhances signal-to-noise for attention.</p>
<h3>Clinical Significance</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>ADHD</strong> — Alpha-2A agonists (guanfacine) enhance PFC noradrenergic function</li>
<li><strong>PTSD</strong> — LC hyperactivation drives hyperarousal symptoms</li>
<li><strong>Depression</strong> — SNRIs target both serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake</li>
</ul>
