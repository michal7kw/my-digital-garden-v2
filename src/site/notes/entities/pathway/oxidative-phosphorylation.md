<h1>Oxidative Phosphorylation</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>The mitochondrial process that couples electron transport from NADH and FADH2 to oxygen with ATP synthesis. Produces the vast majority (~90%) of cellular ATP through chemiosmotic coupling across the inner mitochondrial membrane.</p>
