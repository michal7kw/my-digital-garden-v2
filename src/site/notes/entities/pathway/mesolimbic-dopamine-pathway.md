<h1>Mesolimbic Dopamine Pathway</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Major dopamine pathway from the ventral tegmental area (VTA) to the nucleus accumbens and limbic structures. Central to reward processing, motivation, and reinforcement learning. Dysregulation underlies addiction and anhedonia.</p>
<h3>Clinical Significance</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Addiction</strong> — Drugs of abuse hijack this pathway, causing supraphysiological DA release</li>
<li><strong>Depression</strong> — Reduced mesolimbic activity correlates with anhedonia</li>
<li><strong>Schizophrenia</strong> — Hyperactivity hypothesis for positive symptoms</li>
</ul>
