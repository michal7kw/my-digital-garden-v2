<h1>Mitophagy</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Mitophagy is the selective degradation of damaged or dysfunctional mitochondria by autophagy. It is a critical quality control mechanism that maintains cellular health by removing mitochondria that could otherwise produce excessive reactive oxygen species or trigger apoptosis.</p>
