<h1>Citric Acid Cycle</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>The central metabolic hub that oxidizes acetyl-CoA to CO2, generating NADH, FADH2, and GTP for ATP production. Also known as the TCA cycle or Krebs cycle, it connects carbohydrate, fat, and protein metabolism and provides biosynthetic precursors.</p>
