<h1>Cingulate Cortex</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Medial cortical structure forming a collar around the corpus callosum. The anterior cingulate (ACC) is critical for error detection and conflict monitoring; posterior cingulate for self-referential processing.</p>
<h3>Subdivisions</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Anterior cingulate (ACC)</strong> — Error/conflict monitoring, emotional processing</li>
<li><strong>Posterior cingulate (PCC)</strong> — Self-referential processing, default mode network</li>
<li><strong>Midcingulate</strong> — Motor planning, pain processing</li>
</ul>
