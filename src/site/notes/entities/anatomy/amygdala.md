<h1>Amygdala</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Almond-shaped limbic structure critical for emotional processing, particularly fear conditioning and threat detection. Bidirectionally connected with prefrontal cortex for emotion regulation.</p>
<h3>Nuclei</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Basolateral complex</strong> — Sensory input processing, fear learning</li>
<li><strong>Central nucleus</strong> — Autonomic and behavioral fear output</li>
<li><strong>Medial nucleus</strong> — Olfactory and social behavior processing</li>
</ul>
