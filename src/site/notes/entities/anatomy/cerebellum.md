<h1>Cerebellum</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Contains over half the brain's neurons despite being 10% of brain volume. Essential for motor coordination, balance, timing, and increasingly recognized for cognitive and emotional processing.</p>
<h3>Key Cell Types</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Purkinje cells</strong> — Primary output neurons (GABAergic)</li>
<li><strong>Granule cells</strong> — Most numerous neurons in the brain</li>
<li><strong>Climbing fibers</strong> — Error signals from inferior olive</li>
<li><strong>Mossy fibers</strong> — Sensory/motor input relay</li>
</ul>
