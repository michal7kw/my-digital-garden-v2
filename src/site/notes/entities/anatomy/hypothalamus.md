<h1>Hypothalamus</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Master regulator of homeostasis, controlling body temperature, hunger, thirst, circadian rhythms, and the endocrine system via the pituitary gland. Small but critical diencephalic structure.</p>
<h3>Key Nuclei</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Suprachiasmatic nucleus</strong> — Circadian pacemaker</li>
<li><strong>Paraventricular nucleus</strong> — CRH/oxytocin/vasopressin</li>
<li><strong>Arcuate nucleus</strong> — Appetite regulation</li>
<li><strong>Lateral hypothalamus</strong> — Orexin/feeding center</li>
<li><strong>Ventromedial</strong> — Satiety center</li>
</ul>
