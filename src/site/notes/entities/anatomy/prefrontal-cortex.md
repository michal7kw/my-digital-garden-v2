<h1>Prefrontal Cortex</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Anterior portion of the frontal lobe, the most evolved region of the human brain. Mediates executive function, decision-making, personality expression, and social behavior.</p>
<h3>Subdivisions</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Dorsolateral PFC (dlPFC)</strong> — Working memory, cognitive control</li>
<li><strong>Ventrolateral PFC (vlPFC)</strong> — Response inhibition, language</li>
<li><strong>Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)</strong> — Reward valuation, decision-making</li>
<li><strong>Medial PFC (mPFC)</strong> — Self-referential processing, social cognition</li>
<li><strong>Frontopolar cortex</strong> — Multitasking, future planning</li>
</ul>
