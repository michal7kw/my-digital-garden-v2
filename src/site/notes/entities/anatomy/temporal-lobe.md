<h1>Temporal Lobe</h1>
<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Lateral cortical lobe beneath the Sylvian fissure. Houses the auditory cortex, Wernicke's area for language comprehension, and the medial temporal structures (hippocampus, amygdala) for memory.</p>
